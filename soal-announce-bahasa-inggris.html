6. explain in detail what is announce ment text​


Jawaban:

Definition of Announcement text :

An announcement is a written or spoken statement in public or formal words containing information about an event that has happened or is going to happen so publicly people know what, when, and where it is about.

Purpose of Announcement text :

An announcement is used for giving people some information of what has happened or what will happen.

The purpose of an announcement text or announcement text is to inform people of some information about what has happened or what event is about to take place.

Generic structure of Announcement text :

In the announcement text , there are 4 (four) aspects of the text structure that must be known. The text structure of the announcement text includes:

- Stating Purpose

The text that contains what event will be held - A sentence that provides information about what event will take place or be held.

- Status day and date

Day and date realization. The text that contains when the event will be held - A sentence that provides information about when the event will be held.

- Stating place

The text that contains where the event will be held - A sentence that provides information about the location or place where the event will take place.

- Informing sender

The text that contains the name of the person who will be contacted - A sentence that provides information about the name of someone who can be contacted if there is something that needs to be asked about the announced event.

Language features of Announcement text :

There are 2 (two) linguistic elements that need to be considered in the announcement text , including:

- Using simple present tense and simple future tense

Example sentences used in announcement text

1. Due to some technical problems, the show will...

2. We apologize for the inconvenience.

3. Hi Guys! Our neighborhood will hold a fund Raising Activity next month.

4. The event will be held on May 21st , 2019.

5. The ceremony will be held in the hall of SMP 02 Sukamaju.

- Using exact noun

Examples of sentences used are as follows :

1. All students of SMP Pelita Jaya

2. All eighth grade students' of SMP Sukamaju

3. Mrs. Rose (as the committee)

25. The participants have had some problems deciding.....A. when they should announce the result of the meeting.B. when are they going to announce the result of the meeting.C. When should they announce the result of the meeting..D. the time when the result of the meeting to announce.​


Jawaban:

A

Penjelasan:

subject dulu terus auxilliary/modal/to be

NOUN CLAUSE

The participants have had some problems deciding when they should announce the result of the meeting. (A)

------------

EXPLANATION

Noun clause adalah sebuah dependent clause (anak kalimat) yang berfungsi sebagai kata benda.

Noun clause dapat digantikan dengan kata ganti 'it'.

Noun clause diawali dengan how, that, what, whatever, when, where, if, whether, which, whichever, who, whoever, whom, whomever, atau why.

Meskipun diawali dengan kata tanya, noun clause bukanlah kalimat tanya (interrogative) melainkan pernyataan (declarative). Oleh karena itu, konstruksi noun clause adalah:

QW + S + v bukan QW + auxiliary verb + S + verb

Contoh:

I know when he will come.  ✓

I know when will he come. X

-----------

EXPLICATION

Setelah memahami apa itu noun clause, mari kita bahas satu persatu opsi jawaban:

A. when they should announce the result of the meeting.

declarative (S + verb)  → noun clause

B. when are they going to announce the result of the meeting.

interrogative (auxiliary + S)  → bukan noun clause

C. When should they announce the result of the meeting.

interrogative (auxiliary + S)  → bukan noun clause

D. the time when the result of the meeting to announce.

Bukan klausa karena tidak mempunyai finite verb.
