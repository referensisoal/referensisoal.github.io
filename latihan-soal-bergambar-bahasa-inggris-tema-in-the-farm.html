6. In a farm, 3/8 of the animals were horses. The farmer bought 6 more horses. He then had 12 horses in total. How many animals were there in the farm at first?


Jawaban:

Let's call the total number of animals in the farm initially "x." Given that 3/8 of the animals were horses, we can write the equation (3/8)x = number of horses.

After the farmer bought 6 more horses, he had 12 horses in total. So, the equation becomes (3/8)x + 6 = 12.

how many animals were there in the farm at first = 16

Penjelasan

number of animals in the farm at first = x

number of horses in the farm = 3/8 x x

= 3/8 x x + 6

= 12

= 3/8 x x = 6

= 16

30. The farmers are growing the wheat in the farm ubah ke dalam kalimat passive .. tolong d jawab


Jawaban:

the wheat is being grown by the farmers in the farm.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

The wheat is being grown by the farmers in the farm

PEMBAHASAN

The farmers are growing the wheat in the farm

=> Para petani sedang menumbuhkan padi di ladang

Keterangan:

kalimat tersebut adalah Present Continuous karena menggunakan Be (are) + Verb-ing (growing)

Berikut Rumus Yang digunakan:

Active-Present Continuous

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V-ing + O + C

=> The farmers are growing the wheat in the farm

Penjelasan:

Subject “the farmers” sedang melakukan aksi “are growing”

Keterangan: Subject => The farmers Be => areVerb-ing => growing Object => the wheat Complement => in the farm

Passive-Present Continuous

(+) O + is/am/are + being + V3 + By S + C

=> The wheat is being grown by the farmers in the farm

Penjelasan:

Object “the wheat” diberi aksi/tindakan “is being grown” oleh Subjek “by the farmers”

Keterangan; Object => The wheatTobe => isBeing + V3 => being grownBy Subjek => by the farmers Complement => in the farm
