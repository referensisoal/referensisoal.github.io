1. No.Date :A Briefhistory of Singapore​


Jawaban:

Ancient Malay legend holds that Singapore was founded when a Sumatran prince encountered a lion - considered a good omen - on Temasek. This gave rise to Singapura, or the 'Lion City'. This then became a minor trading post for the Sumatran Srivijaya empire and subsequently a vassal state of the Javanese Majapahit empire in the mid-13th century. In 1819, Sir Stamford Raffles turned his sights on Singapore it emerged as major a tariff-free port and a flourishing colony with a military and naval base.

By the 1950s, a number of political parties were clamouring for independence and Singapore finally attained self-government when the The People's Action Party was elected in 1959. Lee Kuan Yew became prime minister and he held on to this position for the next 31 years. In 1963, Singapore formed a union with Malaya (now Malaysia) but, by 1965, the nascent federation was in tatters. Singapore became independent soon after and was once again the economic success story of the region.

Singapore was hit hard by the economic troubles of the late 90s but it is slowly bouncing back. It remains a city-country renowned for its cleanliness, modernity and efficiency.

25. kata sejarah berasal dari bahasa inggris history yang berarti....


Dalam Bahasa Inggris, sejarah disebut 'history' yang berarti laporan atau catatan mengenai suatu tindakan di masa lampau. Kata 'history' berasal dari Bahasa Yunani 'historia'.

Pembahasan

Sejarah berasal dari kata 'syajarah' dalam Bahasa Arab yang berarti pohon. Hal ini dapat kita maknai sebagai pohon keluarga atau silsilah yang menjelaskan asal-usul terjadinya sesuatu.

Dalam Bahasa Inggris, sejarah disebut 'history'. Kata ini berasal dari Bahasa Yunani 'historia' yang berarti laporan atau catatan mengenai suatu tindakan yang telah lalu. Kata ini kemudian banyak diadopsi ke bahasa lain melalui perantara Bahasa Latin.

Pengertian sejarah menurut KBBI adalah pengetahuan atau penjelasan mengenai suatu kejadian yang benar-benar berlangsung di masa lalu. Berikut adalah pengertian sejarah menurut beberapa ahli:

Thomas Carlyle

Menurut Carlyle, sejarah adalah peristiwa masa lalu yang mempelajari biografi tokoh-tokoh terkenal di zaman tersebut. Orang-orang yang biografinya kita pelajari dalam sejarah memiliki peran besar yang dicatat sebagai peletak dasar sejarah.

Ibnu Kaldun

Ibnu Kaldun berpendapat bahwa sejarah adalah catatan peradaban manusia dan dunia. Sejarah menjelaskan perubahan apa saja yang terjadi sepanjang waktu dan dialami oleh masyarakat tersebut.

Roeslan Abdulgani

Beliau berpendapat bahwa sejarah adalah ilmu yang serupa dengan penglihatan 3 dimensi. Hal ini karena dalam sejarah, kita melihat ke masa lalu, masa sekarang, dan masa depan.

Roeslan Abdulgani berpendapat bahwa mempelajari peristiwa-peristiwa masa lalu dapat membuat kita lebih memahami keadaan kita di masa sekarang. Selain itu, kita juga jadi memiliki perspektif lebih luas tentang masa depan.

29. buatlah cerita naratif bahasa inggris dengan tema history of hologram


Jawaban:

Title: "The Evolution of Holograms"

Long ago, in the early 20th century, a brilliant scientist named Dennis Gabor dreamt of a revolutionary technology that could capture and display three-dimensional images in thin air. Little did he know that his dream would pave the way for the fascinating history of holograms.

In the 1940s, Gabor's research on electron microscopy led him to develop the concept of holography. He envisioned a technique that could record not just the intensity of light but also its phase, allowing for the recreation of lifelike 3D images. However, the technology of the time was not advanced enough to bring his vision to life.

It wasn't until the 1960s that scientists, like Emmett Leith and Juris Upatnieks, made significant breakthroughs in holography. They discovered how to create holograms using lasers, which produced clear and realistic 3D images. Holography quickly gained popularity in the scientific and artistic communities.

As technology advanced, holograms began to find applications in various fields. In the 1970s and 1980s, holograms were used for security purposes, like holographic seals on credit cards and passports. They added a layer of protection against counterfeiting.

In the 1990s, holograms took center stage in the entertainment industry. Musicians like Michael Jackson incorporated holographic performances into their concerts, bringing deceased artists back to life on stage. These spectacles left audiences in awe.

The 21st century witnessed even more incredible developments. Holographic technology advanced to the point where it could be used in medical imaging, helping doctors visualize complex structures inside the human body with greater precision.

But perhaps the most significant milestone came with the advent of augmented and virtual reality. Holograms were no longer confined to static displays; they could now interact with the real world. This breakthrough transformed industries like education, gaming, and medicine, enhancing learning experiences and medical simulations.

Today, holographic technology continues to evolve. From holographic displays in our smartphones to lifelike holographic telepresence for remote communication, the history of holograms has been a journey of innovation and discovery. What was once a dream of a visionary scientist has become an integral part of our technological landscape, offering endless possibilities for the future.
