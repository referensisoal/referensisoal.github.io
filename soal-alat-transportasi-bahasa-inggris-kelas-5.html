Macam-macam alat transportasi air dalam bahasa Inggris yaitu

Ship = kapal Boat = perahuRaft = rakit Submarine = kapal selamCanoe = kanoYacht = kapal pesiarSailing boat = kapal layarFerry = kapal feriPembahasan

Untuk penulisan kapal feri dalam bahasa Inggris, cukup ditulis dengan ferry saja, tidak perlu ditambahkan kata 'boat'.

Pada kamus bahasa Inggris, definisi ferry adalah

A ferry is a form of transport across water.

15. Buatlah 1 paragraf dlm bahasa Inggris tentang alat transportasi darat seperti motor


Buses, as a form of transportation, play a vital role in connecting communities and facilitating the movement of people across cities and regions. With their large seating capacity, buses offer an efficient and cost-effective means of public transportation. They provide a reliable option for daily commutes, allowing individuals to reach their destinations comfortably and on time. Buses also contribute to reducing traffic congestion and air pollution by consolidating multiple passengers into a single vehicle. Whether it's for daily commuting, sightseeing, or long-distance travel, buses continue to serve as a practical and accessible mode of transportation for people of all walks of life.

The means of transports on land are the transportation which are operated on the land instead of on the water or in the air.
