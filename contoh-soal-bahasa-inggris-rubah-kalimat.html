11. apakah regular dan irregular verb bisa merubah bahasa Inggris?


Bisa. regular verb beraturan sedangkan irregular verb tidak beraturanSaya menambahkan ya.

Bisa, tapi itu dalam artian yan diubah adalah bentuk atau form atau yang lebih jelas lagi systematic-nya. Tapi bukan merubah makna (meaning) sebenarnya.

Kalo perubahan makna tentu saja irregular atau regular verb sudah memiliki ketentuan masing-masing dalam penggunaannya.

Tapi, sebagai contoh drink, drank, dan drunk. Mungkin ini sudah pasti artinya minum, kalo perubahan yang dicari misalnya seperti penggunaan drunk yang artinya mabuk. Ini sebenarnya bukan berubah makna, melainkan penambahan fungsional kata. Begitu. 

P.S:
Mungkin bisa lebih di kerucutkan lagi perubahan seperti apa yang ingin diketahui.

31. bantuin tugas bahasa inggris merubah kalimat aktif ke pasif:(​


PASSIVE VOICE

1. The case will be tried [by the court] next week.

2. He was asked [by his landlord] to move.

3. The house was destroyed [by the fire] yesterday.

4. The refrigerator is being repaired [by the repairman] now.

5. Teacher' students were punished [by the teacher] for not doing homework.

6. Goods were delivered [by me] to my friends

7. The meeting was attended [by the ministers] yesterday.

8. Their homework is going to be done [by them].

9. Computer is practiced [by us] in the lab every Tuesday

10. Computer is taught [by Mr. Deni] every Saturday.

ELABORATION

FUTURE SIMPLE

A [S+will+Verb¹+O]

The court will try the case next week

P [S(O)+will+be+V³+by Agent]

The case will be tried [by the court] next week.

PAST SIMPLE

A [S+Verb²+O]

His landlord asked him to move.The fire destroyed the house yesterday.Teacher punished his students for not doing homework.I delivered goods to my friends.The ministers attended the meeting yesterday.

P [S(O)+to be(was/were)+V³+by Agent]

He was asked [by his landlord] to move.The house was destroyed [by the fire] yesterday.Teacher' students was punished [by the teacher] for not doing homeworkGoods were delivered [by me] to my friends.The meeting was attended [by the ministers] yesterday.

PRESENT PROGRESSIVE

A [S+to be(is/am/are)+Verb-ing+O]

The repairman is repairing the refrigerator now.

P [S(O)+to be(is/am/are)+being+V³+by Agent]

The refrigerator is being repaired [by the repairman] now.

PRESENT SIMPLE

A [S+Verb¹(s/es)+O]

We practice computer in the lab every Tuesday.Mr. Deni teaches computer every Saturday.

P [S(O)+to be (is/am/are)+Verb³+by Agent]

Computer is practiced [by us] in the lab every TuesdayComputer is taught [by Mr. Deni] every Saturday.

34. Bagaimana cara merubah kalimat finite ke non finite dalam bahasa inggris


FILL IN THE BLANKS WITH CORRECT WORDS

 

Jawaban    Pendahuluan  

Pengertian Finite clause adalah: Kalimat yang menggunakan kata kerja langsung, tanpa diikuti modals maupun berbentuk gerund (v-ing). Biasanya berbentuk simple present dan simple past.

Sedangkan Pengertian Non Finite clause adalah: Kalimat yang menggunakan gerund(v-ing), modals dan to be.

Contoh Non Finite clause:

I am going to work in Jakarta.

We can explain it to Mr. Joko.

They should be winning this game.

Contoh Finite Clause:

I work in Jakarta.

We explained it to Mr. Joko.

They won this game.


Pembahasan  

Bagaimana merubah kalimat bahasa inggris ini ke dalam bentuk finite clause :

Non Finite clause : He had his car cleaned

Finite clause : He cleaned his car.

Non Finite Clause : Finding the gates widely open, the thief went inside  

Finite Clause : The thief went inside when he found the gate.

Non Finite Clause : It is healthy to laugh at problems

Finite Clause : Let’s laugh at problems.  

Non Finite Clause : The proposal has been examined today

Finite Clause : Someone examined the proposal today.

Non Finite Clause : Nancy is doing her homework at the moment  

Finite Clause : Nancy does her homework.

 

Kesimpulan

Kesimpulannya, kalimat yang menggunakan modals, to be, gerund adalah Non Finite clasue sedangkan yang menggunakan kata kerja langsung adalah Finite clause. Dari contoh diatas kita dapat melihat perbedaan antara Finite dan Non Finite clause.

36. cara merubah kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif dalam bahasa inggris


Passive Voice

Langkah-langkah (cara) merubah kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif dalam bahasa inggris:

Pilih objek penderita dari kalimat aktif itu  Buatlah objek tersebut menjadi subjek dari kalimat pasifnya  Perhatiakan bentuk tensesnya, subjeknya tunggal atau jamak karena tenses yang di gunakan harus sama  Bentuklah predikatnya dengan to be + Verb 3  

PEMBAHASAN:

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Kalimat aktif yang bisa dirubah menjadi kalimat pasif berbentuk kalimat aktif yang mengandung transitive verbs (kata kerja yang memerlukan objek langsung) seperti make, bring, buy, write dan sebagainya. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif yang mengandung intransitive verbs (kata kerja yang tidak memerlukan objek langsung) seperti cry, swim, go, arrive, dan die tidak bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif. Tetapi, apabila intransitive verbs tersebut diikuti oleh preposition, ada kemungkinan kalimat tersebut bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif tetapi kalimat pasif yang terbentuk akan sangat kaku.

Rumus Kalimat Pasif (Passive Voice)

Subject (object dari kalimat aktif) + to be ( disesuaikan dengan bentuk tensesnya + Verb 3 (past participle)

TENSES                                                 RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE

Simple present                                   subject + is/am/are + Verb 3

Present continuous                      subject + is/am/are + being + Verb 3

Present perfect                               subject + have/has been + Verb 3

Simple past                                          subject + was/were + Verb 3

Past continuous                            subject + was/were + being + Verb 3

Past perfect                                          subject + had been + Verb 3

Simple future                                        subject + will be + Verb 3

Be going to                              subject + is/am/are + going to + be + Verb 3

Future continuous                        subject + will + be + being + Verb 3

Future perfect                                   subject + will have been + Verb 3

Modal                                              Subject + modal + be + Verb 3
