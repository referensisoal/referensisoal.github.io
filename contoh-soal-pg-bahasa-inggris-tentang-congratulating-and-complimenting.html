4. 1. * Indentifity social function congratulating and complimenting others! * The structure congratulating and complimenting others! * Teks linguistik element in the expression congratulating and complimenting others!2. Respond to complementing and complimens other! ​


Congratulating berarti ekspresi yang digunakan untuk memberikan selamat. Sedangkan complementing others berarti ekspresi yang digunakan untuk memuji orang lain.

1. The social function of congratulating is to congratulate someone on his or her achievement.

The social function of complimenting is to compliment someone on his or her appearance.

2. The structure of congratulating is "Congratulation on your ...."

The structure of complimenting:

What a/an [adjective] + [Noun] + [pronoun] + [to be]How [adjective] + the + [Noun] + [to be]

An example of responding to complementing and compliments other:

A; Hi. you look handsome with your new haircut.

B: Thank you. You look so beautiful in this dress.

A; Thank you.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat congratulation dan responsnya yaitu

1. “Congratulations on your wedding. I wish you a happy life ahead.”

Response: “It’s so nice of you to say so.”

2. “Please accept my warmest congratulations on your success.”

Response: “Thank you so much.”

3. “I’d like to congratulate you on opening your new store.”

Response: “Thank you, I really appreciate it.”

10. Can you explain about Congratulating and Complimenting!​


Jawaban:

Compliment is an expression that we show or say to express/give praise.  Some people use compliments to “butter up” somebody or to flatter in order to increase good will. Complement is for anyone you have occasion to talk with (friends, family, colleagues, and even strangers).

Complement is express to praise about:

- the way people’s look/ general appearance

- something people have

- something people did   

- something people said

Congratulation is an expression that we use to give the congratulation utterance when he/she success in doing something.

Kinds of Congratulations:

- Congratulation of someone’s success

- Happy Birthday

- Merry Christmas

- Happy New Year

- Happy Valentine

- Happy Anniversary

31. How is correlation betwen congratulating and complimenting ​


Jawaban:

Expression congratulation adalah ekspresi atau ungkapan yang kita gunakan/ucapkan ketika seseorang meraih keberhasilan atau mencapai sukses dalam melakukan sesuatu.

Contoh expression congratulation :

Congratulation on your wedding anniversary!

Happy Graduation!

Happy Birthday!

Happy New Year!

Merry Christmas!

Happy Eid Mubarak

Expression Compliment adalah ungkapan yang gunakan untuk tujuan memberikan pujian terhadap seseorang, benda atau tempat (Noun). Contoh kalimat expression compliment :

What a lovely place you have!

What a big house it is!

How nice you are!

How smart your students are!

What a magnificent place we visit!

Pembahasan

Let's make a dialogue/conversation based on the situations given

Situation 1

Linda just finished her study and got cumlaude. Lulu was very happy and hope that she will be useful for her country (Congratulations)

Lulu : Hi Linda, Congratulation! You finished your study, I hope that you will be useful for your country!

Linda : Thank you Lulu, that's very nice of you.

Situation 2

Darma just got predicate that he is the best employee in his office. His Dad was very proud of him and hope he will increase his work. (Congratulations)

Dad : Woww...Congratulation Darma! I am very proud of you Son! Keep up the good work!

Darma : Thanks Dad for your appreciation.

Situation 3

Vina's novel was being the best seller in this city for 3 months. Sandra congratulation her and hope she will make a new book soon. (Congratulations)

Vina : Hi Sandra, I am very happy that my novel have been the best seller for 3 months!

Sandra : Congratulation! You deserve it. You've working very hard on this book.

Vina : Thank you

Sandra : I hope you will make a new book soon!

Vina : Thank you Sandra, I am working on the next one at the moment.

Situation 4

Utari won the gold medal on the national singing competition. Winda congratulate her and hope that she will represent Indonesia in the next international singing competition. (Congratulations)

Winda : Hi Utari, I heard you won the gold medal, Congratulation on your achievement!

Utari : Thank you Winda!

Winda : I hope you will represent Indonesia in the next international singing competition.

Utari : Thank you, I hope so!

Situation 5

Mely and Adi were get married.  Sinta congratulate them and hope they will live happily ever after. (Congratulations)

Sinta : Happy wedding for you both! Wish you live happily ever after!

Mely and Adi : Thanks Sinta!

Situation 6

Anya make a beautiful dress for her graduation party. Laura very likes her dress and she said that the beautiful dress match with her beautiful face. (Expressing Compliment)

Laura : Wow...What a beautiful dress you're wearing!

Anya : Thanks Laura, I made it by myself.

Laura : It's absolutely beautiful and match your beautiful face too!

Anya : Wow...thanks for the compliment Laura.

Situation 7

Milka just finished her ballet performance. Vida loved her performance so much, so she hope that she can be like her. (Expression Compliment)

Vida : What an amazing perfomance!! I love it. I hope someday I can be in the ballet performance like you.

Milka : Thank you, Vida. It's nice to hear that you love the performance.

Situation 8

Heri was complete his last practice in High School and get the highest score. His sister was very proud of him. (Expressing compliment)

Sister : How was your practice Heri?

Heri : It was good, I got the highest score!

Sister : Wow..that's really good, I'm very proud of you!

Heri : Thanks

Situation 9

Salma is wearing new skirt on the wedding, Dinda said it was a beautiful skirt she ever saw. (Expressing Compliment)

Dinda : Ooh...What a beautiful skirt you're wearing! That's the most beautiful skirt I ever saw!

Salma : Thank you Dinda, I love it too!

Situation 10

Dendi is a professional photographer, Sandrina likes all her photo taken by him, so she wants him to be her photographer on her birthday party (Expressing Compliment)

Sandrina : I love all my photo that taken by you Dendy,  I would be very happy if you can be my photographer on my birthday.

Dendi : Thanks for the compliment Sandrina, I would love too.

Sandrina : Thanks Dendi.

Kesimpulan

Expression congratulation and expression compliment dalam dialog diatas digunakan untuk mengungkapkan sesuatu disesuaikan dengan situasi yang diberikan.
