11. past continuous tense (+),(-),(?)preset continuous tense (+),(-),(?)future continuous tense (+),(-),(?)contoh:​


Jawaban:

Past continuous tense

(+) I was watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(-) I was not watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(?) Was I watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home  last night?

Preset continuous tense

(+) Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment.

(-) Our school principal is not giving his speech at the moment.

(?) Is our school principal giving his speech at the moment?

Future continuous tense

(+) I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(-) I will not be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(?) Will I be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning?

Penjelasan:

Ketiga tenses ini mengandung kata "continuous" (berkelanjutan/terus-menerus) yang mengandung makna bahwa yang dikerjakan itu masih berlangsung.

Dalam Past continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik di masa lampau. (Dalam contoh si atas adalah "when my mother came home last night" (ketika ibuku pulang tadi malam). Jadi kalimat ini bermakna bahwa ketika ibu pulang tadi malam, saya sedang menonton film . Jadi kegiatan menonton itu masih berlangsung ketika ibu pulang, tadi malam (masa lampau)

Dalam Present continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik masa kini atau sekarang (yaitu pada titik waktu ketika sipenutur mengucapkan kata ini).

Contoh kalimat di atas: Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment. (kepala sekolah saat ini sedang berpidato).

Skenarionya misalnya kamu sedang ada di sekolah mendengarkan pidato kepala sekolah, lalu kamu ditelpon mama, lalu kamu bilang "Mom, our school principal is giving his speech at the moment"

Dalam Future continuous tense, kita membayangkan/memperkirakan bahwa di satu  titik waktu di masa depan sesuatu sedang terjadi/dikerjakan.

Dalam contoh di atas: I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow moring.

Artinya besok pagi pas waktu kamu tiba, saya akan sedang melakukan sesuatu yaitu menyiapkan laporan.

Untuk rumus ada di buku pelajaran.

22. kak tolong kasih tau contoh kalimat positif negatif dan introgatifnya passive voice dari Present continuous tense, Present perfect tense, Present perfect continuous tense, Simple past tense, Past continuous tense, Past perfect tense, Past perfect continuous, dunggg


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh kalimat positif, negatif, dan interrogatif dalam bentuk passive voice dari beberapa tense yang Anda sebutkan:

1. Present Continuous Tense (sekarang sedang terjadi):

- Positif: The cake is being baked by my sister. (Adik perempuan saya sedang memanggang kue.)

- Negatif: The book is not being read by the students. (Buku tidak sedang dibaca oleh para siswa.)

- Interrogatif: Is the car being washed by the mechanic? (Apakah mobil sedang dicuci oleh montir?)

2. Present Perfect Tense (terjadi di masa lampau namun memiliki dampak di masa sekarang):

- Positif: The house has been renovated by the new owners. (Rumah telah direnovasi oleh pemilik baru.)

- Negatif: The report hasn't been submitted by the team yet. (Laporan belum diserahkan oleh tim.)

- Interrogatif: Have the invitations been sent by the event organizer? (Apakah undangan telah dikirim oleh penyelenggara acara?)

3. Present Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi di masa lalu dan masih berlanjut hingga sekarang):

- Positif: The project has been being worked on by the team for several months. (Proyek telah dikerjakan oleh tim selama beberapa bulan.)

- Negatif: The garden hasn't been being watered regularly. (Taman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur.)

- Interrogatif: How long has the patient been being treated by the doctor? (Berapa lama pasien telah dirawat oleh dokter?)

4. Simple Past Tense (terjadi di masa lalu):

- Positif: The movie was watched by millions of people. (Film tersebut ditonton oleh jutaan orang.)

- Negatif: The letter wasn't sent by John. (Surat itu tidak dikirim oleh John.)

- Interrogatif: Was the game played by the children yesterday? (Apakah permainan itu dimainkan oleh anak-anak kemarin?)

5. Past Continuous Tense (sedang terjadi di masa lampau):

- Positif: The car was being repaired by the mechanic when I arrived. (Mobil sedang diperbaiki oleh montir saat saya tiba.)

- Negatif: The phone wasn't being used by anyone when it rang. (Telepon tidak sedang digunakan oleh siapapun saat berdering.)

- Interrogatif: What were you doing when the accident happened? (Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan ketika kecelakaan terjadi?)

6. Past Perfect Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau):

- Positif: The package had been delivered by the courier before I got home. (Paket telah dikirim oleh kurir sebelum saya pulang.)

- Negatif: The task hadn't been completed by the deadline. (Tugas belum selesai sebelum batas waktu.)

- Interrogatif: Had the tickets been booked by the travel agent when you called? (Apakah tiket sudah dipesan oleh agen perjalanan ketika kamu menelepon?)

7. Past Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau dan masih berlanjut):

- Positif: The team had been working on the project for weeks before they finally completed it. (Tim telah bekerja pada proyek tersebut selama berbulan-bulan sebelum akhirnya menyelesaikannya.)

- Negatif: The plants hadn't been being watered regularly before they withered. (Tanaman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur sebelum layu.)

- Interrogatif: How long had they been waiting for the bus before it arrived? (Berapa lama mereka telah menunggu bus sebelum bus itu tiba?)

28. jelaskan dengan jelas tentang past continuous tense beserta contohnya(semua dalam bahasa inggris)​


Past continuous tense or also commonly known as past progressive tense is a tense that is used to express activities that are happening at a certain time in the past.
The action / activity has been and is ongoing but has not been completed at that time and sometimes also interspersed with an action / activity that occurs briefly.

as example ;
1.my mother 'was' cooking fried rice while I 'was' repairing my laptop.

2.'were' yo u working all night yesterday?

3.rina 'wasn't' doing her home work when Santi called her.

4.we 'weren't' playing outside when the teacher 'came'.

39. buatlah 8 contoh kalimat past continuous tense dan preset continuous tense​


Berikut adalah delapan contoh kalimat dalam bentuk Past Continuous Tense dan Present Continuous Tense:

Past Continuous Tense:
1. I was studying for my exam last night.
(Saya sedang belajar untuk ujian saya semalam.)

2. They were playing football in the park when it started raining.
(Mereka sedang bermain sepak bola di taman ketika hujan mulai turun.)

3. She was cooking dinner while he was watching TV.
(Dia sedang memasak makan malam sementara dia sedang menonton TV.)

4. We were walking along the beach when we saw a beautiful sunset.
(Kami sedang berjalan di sepanjang pantai ketika kami melihat matahari terbenam yang indah.)

Present Continuous Tense:
1. I am reading a book right now.
(Saya sedang membaca buku sekarang.)

2. They are having dinner at the restaurant.
(Mereka sedang makan malam di restoran.)

3. She is writing an email to her friend.
(Dia sedang menulis email kepada temannya.)

4. We are watching a movie tonight.
(Kami sedang menonton film malam ini.)

Perhatikan bahwa dalam contoh-contoh di atas, Past Continuous Tense menunjukkan aksi yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau, sedangkan Present Continuous Tense menunjukkan aksi yang sedang berlangsung di saat ini.
