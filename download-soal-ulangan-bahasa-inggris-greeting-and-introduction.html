28. tujuan advertisments, greeting card, announcements, and invitations dalam bahasa inggris?




Purpose advertisements (ads) is a media marketing that aims to encourage the listener or reader to buy or use the goods or services offered in the advertisement.

The purpose greeting cards greeting card means that aims to express feelings or give attention to others.

The purpose announcements are statements (formal notice) in oral or written form that contains something to be known by many people.

Invitation invitations purpose is given by one person to another to attend an important event, such as student council meeting, someone's birthday celebration, school graduation, farewell after graduate school, or an institution.
