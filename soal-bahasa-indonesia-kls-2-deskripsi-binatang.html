1. deskripsi kan cerita tentang kebun binatang dalam bahasa inggris dan indonesia


Deskripsi tentang kebun binatang dalam bahasa Inggris

A zoo is a place, indoor or outdoor where animals especially wild animals such as lion, tiger, cheetah and etc are lived and kept for public exhibition. The word zoo comes from zoology. There are many kinds of the zoo, such as safari parks, aquarium, petting zoos, and animal theme parks. Safari Park is a big outdoor animal park, the visitor allow to come near the animals and can feed the through the window car.

A person who works in the zoo is called zookeeper. Their jobs are to feed the animals, maintain and clean the zoo.  

Zoo is very important many ways, it educates people about animals and biodiversity.

Deskripsi dalam bahasa Indonesia:

Kebun binatang adalah suatu tempat, baik indoor ataupun outdoor dimana hewan liar seperti singa, harimau, cheetah dan lain sebagainya hidup dan digunakan untuk pameran. Kata zoo berasal dari kata zoology. Ada beberapa macam kebun binatang, seperti taman safari, akuarium, petting zoo dan theme park. Taman safari adalah kebun binatang outdoor, dimana pengunjung diperbolehkan masuk mendekati hewan disana dan dapat memberi makan melalui kaca jendela.

Orang yang bekerja di kebun binatang disebut zookeeper. Tugasnya adalah memberi makan hewan, mengurus dan membersihkan kebun binatang.

Keberadaan kebun binatang sangatlah penting, untuk memberikan pengetahuan mengenai hewan dan keanekaragamannya.

Penjelasan

Descriptive text adalah suatu text yang menjelaskan atau memberikan gambaran (deskripsi) mengenai seseorang, binatang, suatu benda atau suatu tempat. Jadi, tujuan dari descriptive text adalah untuk menggambarkan suatu hal.

Ciri-ciri dari descriptive text diantaranya:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan action verb yaitu kata kerja yang menunjukkan suatu kegiatan yang bisa dilihat, contohnya walk, sleep, read, cook dan sebagainya.Verb yang digunakan yaitu attribute verb, seperti be (am, is, are)

9. tolong buatkan percakapan dalam bahasa inggris tentang mendeskripsikan suatu binatang


Situation: James is walking in the park with his dog. He meets Jessica at the park. She is with her cat. Then they are talking about animal and their hobbies. Let’s check the conversation below!

James: Hey, Jesica. Wow! I never thought that we will meet at the park with your pet.

Jesica: This is impressive, isn’t it?

James: Yes, it is. How long have you been here with the pet?

Jesica: Well. I’ve taken care of cats since last month. This is my third cat. The name is Cathy. She came from Persia.

James: Oh, man! She is cute, isn’t she?

Jesica: Yes, she is.

Jesica: She is three months old. She was 2 months when I bought her in the pet shop.

James: Wow!

Jesica: So, who is that dog, James?

James: The name is Jericho. He comes from bulldog family. I took him when he was a baby. Now he is five month.

Jesica: How many dogs do you have, James?

James: I have four dogs at my house and three dogs in my uncle’s house.

Jesica: Wow! That’s amazing, James.

James: Yeah. I can’t take care all of them in my house, so I need my uncle hands to take care the dogs.

Jesica: Are they the bulldogs?

James: This is the only one. The others are Siberian Husky and Doberman.

Jesica: Are they tame?

James: Yes, they are. But keep your eyes open because they can hurt you someday. It means that we have to love them in every situation and condition. If we can do it well, I’m sure they will do the same thing to us.

Jesica: Yes, I agree with that. Do you agree that we have to protect them from the bad guys?

James: Yes, I agree with you. We have to protect them from the black market seller.

Jesica: Yes, you’re right. How many times do you give them foods?

Jesica: Yes, I do the same thing with you. However, they are a part of our family. Am I right?

James: Yes, you’re right. By the way, the sun is going down. We have to go home. See you tomorrow in here, Jesica.

Jesica: See you soon, James.
