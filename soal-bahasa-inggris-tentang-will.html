8. diliat dong soal aku:'(buatlah minimal 20 kalimat Will/would dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris, dengan rumus = (-)is + will/would + not + v1.TOLONG DIJAWAB


Contoh kalimat simple future tense biasa kita pakai dalam kehidupan sehari-hari ketika membicarakan hal yang akan terjadi atau rencana.

Kalimat simple future tense ditandai dengan will + V1 dan to be + going to.

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris simple future tense:

(+) I will call the police if they try to break into this building. / Aku akan menelepon polisi jika mereka mencoba masuk ke gedung ini.

(-) I won’t call the police if they don’t try to break into this building. / Aku akan menelepon polisi jika mereka mencoba masuk ke gedung ini.

(?) Will you call the police if they try to break into this building? / Apakah kamu akan menelepon polisi jika mereka mencoba masuk ke gedung ini?

(+) My mother will buy me a new bicycle. / Ibuku akan membelikanku sepeda baru.

(-) My mother will not buy me a new bicycle. / Ibuku tidak akan membelikanku sepeda baru.

(?) Will your mother buy you a new bicycle? / Apakah ibumu akan membelikanmu sepeda baru?

(+) I will use another product. / Aku akan menggunakan produk lain.

(-) I won’t use another product. / Aku tidak akan menggunakan produk lain.

(?) Will you use another product? / Apakah kamu akan menggunakan produk lain?

(+) I will help you to solve the problem. / Aku akan membantumu memecahkan masalahnya.

(-) I won’t help you to solve the problem. / Aku tidak akan membantumu memecahkan masalahnya.

(?) Will you help me to solve the problem? / Akankah (maukah) kamu membantuku memecahkan masalahnya?

(+) She will text me tomorrow. / Dia akan mengirimiku pesan besok.

(-) She won’t text me tomorrow. / Dia tidak akan mengirimiku pesan besok.

(?) Will she text you tomorrow? / Apakah dia akan mengirimimu pesan besok?

(+) My brother is going to Bali tomorrow. / Kakakku akan pergi ke Bali besok.

(-) My brother isn’t going to Bali tomorrow. / Kakakku akan pergi ke Bali besok.

(?) Is your brother going to Bali tomorrow? / Apakah kakakmu akan pergi ke Bali besok?

(+) I’m going to bake cookies tonight because there is a lot of eggs and brown sugar. / Aku akan membuat kue malam ini karena ada banyak telur dan gula coklat.

(-) I’m not going to bake cookies tonight because there is a lot of eggs and brown sugar. / Aku tidak akan membuat kue malam ini karena ada banyak telur dan gula coklat.

(?) Are you going to bake cookies tonight because there is a lot of eggs and brown sugar? / Apakah kamu akan membuat kue malam ini karena ada banyak telur dan gula coklat?

(+) I’m going to spend my holiday at my grandmother’s house. / Aku akan menghabiskan liburanku di rumah nenekku.

(-) I’m not going to spend my holiday at my grandmother’s house. / Aku tidak akan menghabiskan liburanku di rumah nenekku.

(?) Are you going to spend your holiday at your grandmother’s house? / Apakah kamu akan menghabiskan liburanmu di rumah nenekmu?

(+) Sonya is going to write another story. / Sonya akan menulis cerita lain.

(-) Sonya is not going to write another story. / Sonya tidak akan menulis cerita lain.

(?) Is Sonya going to write another story? / Apakah Sonya akan menulis cerita lain?

(+) Tom is going to watch a movie since his brother gave him free ticket. / Tom akan menonton film karena kakaknya memberinya tiket gratis.

(-) Tom is not going to watch a movie since his brother didn’t give him free ticket. / Tom tidak akan menonton film karena kakaknya tidak memberinya tiket gratis.

(?) Is Tom going to watch a movie since his brother gave him free ticket? / Apakah Tom akan menonton film karena kakaknya memberinya tiket gratis?

[tex]{\bold{Explanation:}}[/tex]

Future Tense :

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

Future Tense dibagi menjadi dua :

willto be going to

Form :

will :

(+) S + will + V1 + O/C(-) S + will + not + V1 + O/C(?) Will + S + V1 + O/C + ?

to be going to :

(+) S + to be + going to + V1 + O/C(-) S + to be + not + going to + V1 + O/C(?) to be + S + going to + V1 + O/C + ?

Keterangan :

S : Subject (Subjek) ; I, you, they, we, she, he, it, etc.V1 : Infinitive, e. g : go, ride, sleep, eat, drink, walk, etcO : Object (Objek)C : Compliment (Keterangan)to be : is, am, are

Note :

Gunakan to be am untuk subjek I.Gunakan to be is untuk subjek she, he, it, dan benda/objek tunggal, misal : Dog, Lina, Rani, Father, etc.Gunakan subjek are untuk subjek you, they, we, dan benda/objek jamak, misal : you and I, Rina and Lani, etc.

Hope it helps

29. membuat dialog bahasa inggrisdari soal berikut ini1. your friends is getting influenza2. your sister Will hold a birthday party tonight3. your brother Will have a national examination next week​


Jawaban:

(1)  Your friends is getting influenza

A: Hey, how are you feeling?

B: Not so good, I think I'm coming down with something.

A: Oh no, what is it?

B: I think it's the flu. My throat is sore and I have a fever.

A: That doesn't sound good. Have you seen a doctor?

B: No, not yet. But I've been resting and taking over-the-counter medicines for the symptoms.

A: Take care of yourself and drink plenty of fluids. Is there anything else you need?

B: Thank you for your concern. Can you help me get some groceries? It's hard for me to go out in this condition.

A: Of course! Just send me the list, and I'll get everything for you.

B: That would be a great help. Thank you so much!

A: Don't mention it. Take care and get well soon!

B: Thanks again, I really appreciate it.

(2) Your sister Will hold a birthday party tonight

Person A: Hey, did you hear that your sister is going to hold a birthday party tonight?

Person B: Oh really? That's great news! Where is she having the party?

Person A: She's hosting it at our house. Mom and dad agreed to let her use the backyard for the celebration.

Person B: That sounds like a fun location! Is there going to be any special theme or decorations?

Person A: Yes, my sister decided on a Hawaiian luau theme. So, expect to see lots of tropical decorations and maybe even some grass skirts!

Person B: Awesome! I love Hawaiian-themed parties. What time does the party start?

Person A: The guests are supposed to arrive around 7 p.m., so we should probably get everything ready beforehand.

Person B: Definitely, we don't want to keep everyone waiting. Are there any specific activities or games planned for the party?

Person A: My sister has organized some traditional Hawaiian games like Limbo and Hula Hoop contests. It should be entertaining for everyone.

Person B: Sounds like a blast! Do you need any help with setting up or preparing anything before the guests arrive?

Person A: Actually, yes! It would be great if you could help us set up some tables and chairs in the backyard. We also need assistance with hanging up decorations.

Person B: Of course! I'll be glad to lend a hand in making sure everything looks perfect for your sister's special day.

(3) Your brother Will have a national examination next week

A: Hey, I heard that your brother will have a national examination next week. How is he preparing for it?

B: Yes, you're right! He's been studying really hard. He's been attending extra classes and spending long hours revising his notes.

A: That's great to hear! Is he feeling confident about the exam?

B: Well, he is a bit nervous because this exam is quite important for his future academic pursuits. But overall, he feels well-prepared and determined to do his best.

A: I'm sure he'll do great! Does he have any specific strategies or study techniques that he's using?

B: Absolutely! He has created a study schedule to manage his time efficiently. He breaks down each subject into smaller topics and focuses on them one by one. Additionally, he practices past exam papers to get familiar with the format and types of questions.

A: That sounds like a solid plan. Is there anything we can do to support him during this crucial time?

B: Definitely! Encouragement and moral support mean a lot to him. We can remind him of how capable he is and offer help whenever needed, whether it's proofreading essays or quizzing him on different subjects.

A: That's wonderful. It shows how much you care about his success. I wish your brother all the best for his national examination!

B: Thank you so much for your kind words! I'll pass on your wishes to him.
