22. 2.tugas bahasa Inggris kelas 9​


Jawaban:

15). a. Morinda citrifolia in general

16). d. dark green

17). a. can be consumed by the human being

18). a. strong

19). b. is located

20). b. were choosen

Penjelasan:

15). the answer is a, because the text is not only explaining Morinda citrifolia 's size, origin, and it's flowers.

16). the answer is d, because one of the sentences in the text said "It can grow up to 9 inches tall, and has large, simple, dark green, shiny, and deeply veined leaves."

17). the answer is a, because the last paragraph said that Southeast Asians and Australians Aborigines consume the fruit raw with salt or cook it with curry. It means that the fruit of Morinda citrifolia can be consumed by humans.

18). the answer is a, because 'pungent' has the closest meaning with 'strong'.

19). the answer is b, because the subject in the question is a singular subject and it also not included into past tense. So we can choose ' is located' instead of 'was', 'are', and 'were'.

20). the answer is b, because Sandrina and Lupita are included as plural subject and the question is using past tense. So we can choose ' were choosen' instead of 'are', 'will be', and 'is being'.

33. BAHASA INGGRISKELAS 8 SEMESTER 2​


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

1. Fish does not fly, they swim.

2. the sun does not rise in the west. It sets in the west.

3. A spider does not have wings. A bird has wings.

4. Cats do not bark, dogs do.

5. Natalie does not usually sit in the sun. She rarely goes to the beach.

6. Babies do not talk, they cry.

7 Dalia often dries the dishes, but she does not put them away.

8. Betty does not usually do her homework before she eats supper.

9. Sari brushes her hair every morning before she goes to school.

10. Nirwan always tries to be a good boy, but he does not behave well.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan sehari hari dalam Bahasa Inggris.

1. Ikan tidak terbang, mereka berenang.

2. matahari tidak terbit di barat. Itu (matahari) terbenam di barat.

3. Laba-laba tidak memiliki sayap. Seekor burung memiliki sayap.

4. Kucing tidak mengg0ngg0ng, guguk melakukannya.

5. Natalie biasanya tidak duduk di bawah sinar matahari. Dia jarang pergi ke pantai.

6. Bayi tidak berbicara, mereka menangis.

7 Dalia sering mengeringkan piring, tetapi dia tidak menyimpannya.

8. Betty biasanya tidak mengerjakan PR sebelum makan malam.

9. Sari menyisir rambutnya setiap pagi sebelum berangkat sekolah.

10. Nirwan selalu berusaha menjadi anak yang baik, tapi dia tidak berperilaku baik.

Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

(-) Subject + Do/Does + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

39. Situation 2 bahasa inggris kelas 9


Situation 1:

Lina has just got the result of her Math test. She is waiting for the result of  her English test. Dayu congratulates her for the result of the Math test and hope  that she will get an A for the English test, too.

The conversation:

Lina: “Thank God, I got an A for my math test. But, I’m not sure of the  result of my English test.”

Dayu: “Congratulations for the result of your Math test. I hope you will get  an A for the English test too.”

Situation 2:

Dayu is going to participate in the bike race to celebrate the Independence  Day. Siti shows her hope that Dayu will get a prize.

The conversation:

Dayu: “Siti, wish me luck. I will take part in the bike race to celebrate the  Independence Day.”

Siti: “Sure. Good luck. I hope you will win the race.

Situation 3:

Edo’s father has given him permission to walk to the mountain. Beni is happy  for him and expresses his hope that he will have a safe trip.

The conversation:

Edo: “Thank God. Finally my father lets me go to the mountain walk.”

Beni: “I’m happy for you. Have a safe trip.”

Situation 4:

Lina tells Udin that she will get a scholarship from the government to finish  her SMP. Udin congratulates her and wish her for the best with her study.

The conversation:

Lina: “Udin, I will get a scholarship from the government to finish my  SMP.”

Udin: “Congratulations! I wish you all the best with your study.”

Situation 5:

Beni has ful!lled all the requirements to run for the OSIS Chairman. Udin is  happy for him and hope that he will be the next OSIS Chairman.

The conversation:

Beni: “The vice pterperincipal said that I had all the requirements to run  for the OSIS Chairman.”

Udin: “I’m happy for you. I hope you will be our next OSIS Chairman.”

Pembahasan

Congratulation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan ucapan selamat kepada orang lain atas pencapaian yang telah diraihnya.

Beberapa contoh ucapan congratulation adalah:

"Congratulations for winning basket competition” “This calls for celebrating! Congratulations!” “You did it! So proud of you!” “I knew it was only a matter of time. Well done!” “You’ve worked so hard for this. Congrats!” “This is awesome! You’re awesome! Way to go!” “Sincere congratulations on your hard-earned success.” Pelajari Lebih Lanjut Contoh kalimat congratulation dan responnya - https://brainly.co.id/tugas/938858 Detil Jawaban
