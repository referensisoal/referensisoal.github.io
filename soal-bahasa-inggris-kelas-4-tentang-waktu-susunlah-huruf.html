25. Menyusun huruf acak ke dalam bahasa Inggris K_E_


❤Answer✅

Keen

❄️Explanation❄️

Keen commonly means eager, interested, or enthusiastic. This sense of the word is often followed by about or on and the particular interest, as in He's very keen on music. More generally, keen means sharp, intense, or strong.
