Assalamualikum wr. wb.

Peace be upon you, and Allah mercy and blessings.

In the atmosphere of Indonesia’s independence day, I want to inflame the spirit of Indonesian independence, which seems, it has eroded on the souls of Indonesian youth. We, as the Indonesian people, certainly still remember how great the struggles of our heroes were in fighting for independence from the hands of the colonialists. Their services will be priceless.

As a great nation, we must be able to appreciate the services of our heroes. However, what is more important at this time is how can we fill independence? Filling this independence we can do with various activities in accordance with our respective fields to lead to the ideals of independence which are essentially the ideals of heroes, namely the formation of a just and prosperous social life.

We live in an age as a generation who carry the mandate of freedom fighters to fill independence as well as possible. The role of young people like us is very much needed in order to fight the enemies of the nation and state. We have great potential in bringing this ark of Indonesian leadership to the fore. Then, what kind of struggles can we do as you young? There are many things we can do to fill independence, one of which is by studying hard for the sake of ideals and making the nation proud.

My friends, what our nation is facing today is really hard. In an uncertain domestic political, economic, and social situation, it forces us to take part in guarding the running of the wheels of the nation and state government. Therefore, let us fight together for the nation and state of Indonesia with the things we have, with the things we are able to do, and with all our souls and bodies to serve the nation.

My fellow countrymen and homeland, let us continue the spirit of our heroes with the spirit of development. Because development is also a struggle that is no less important than the struggle for independence. What does it mean to be free if life gets more miserable?

Once again, let’s fill in the commemoration of independence day by increasing the spirit of struggle by realizing the spirit of development to fill independence.

Okay friends, I hope that is a few words that I can say. Hopefully it can make us all as a young generation who are tough and modest in filling Indonesia’s independence. Because actually filling and defending independence is more difficult than winning independence. As said Ir. I hope that is what I can say, there is something less and less I apologize sincerely.

Finally, let us continue to pray and struggle. Allah is with us, Merdeka !!!!!!!

32. buat karangan bahasa inggris bertema my school life


My school is different from other schools and I love my school. I am penning down my thoughts on what makes my school different……..


Can you think of the place, where one meets children from different backgrounds, temperaments, some mischievous, some bright, some really cute.

The challenge of discovering for yourself the magic of science in the laboratories, learning the skill of programming, making robots dancing to your tune in the computer laboratory, makes you feel like a magician.

Could there be a better fairy land than a school which makes it possible for us to create all this? cool, where one makes friends, one plays, enjoys, learns and grows. Such a place, my friends, can only be a school!

Imagine how dull our lives would be if there were no schools. The open spacious grounds to run and play, the world of books, where one lives in the characters described in them, the experience of living a life with different characters in novels.

Losing oneself in them, visiting different cities, countries, reliving the past, the present and the future, as visualized by the writer, gives a completely different dimension to one’s life. Could it be possible had it not been for the school library?

My school, which is situated in the heart of Delhi, is the place which I have described above. I love my school; it is like the blood which flows in my veins, I cannot live without it. I am what I am because of this wonderful part of my life.

My school is the temple of learning which I worship each day, without fail. It is here that I have learnt to differentiate between right and wrong, to read and enjoy what I read, the skill to manage my conflicts, the ability to modify my behavior, respect my elders and friends, be kind, compassionate, forgiving and good. I learn all this from my teachers who have these qualities and do not just ask me to be good. They make me understand, how to be good, by being good themselves. They never lose their patience, they do not scold, they just make us reflect over what we have done and what will be the consequences of our actions.

In other words they make us accountable for what we do. They forgive us for our mistakes and we learn to forgive others for theirs. When they teach, we are spell bound, we do not know how, but we keep on answering correctly, all the questions, which they put up to us? Learning can be fun is what you can experience in our school. We debate, discuss, question and then conclude. The best part is there are teachers, who learn with us, rather than making us learns.

Who would not want to remain 24 hours in a school like ours? We go on field trips, live in villages, visit historical places, attend leadership camps, participate in adventure camps and are involved in so many activities that life is filled with excitement, *each day.

We have coaching for nearly all games and a state of the art gymnasium where all athletes and sports persons undergo scientifically designed training on a number of workstations.

I think no school has a better sports culture than ours. It is because of this that our school students do exceptionally well at the National and International arena. We are indeed lucky to be studying in a school like ours. My school is fun, my school is exciting and each day in my school is full of opportunities, an adventure, and a discovery.

Oh my dear school I love you so much that I can not imagine my life without you.
