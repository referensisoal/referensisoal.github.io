18. buatlah Identification dan Deskription tentang My School Bag​


Jawaban:

**Identification:**

My school bag is a sturdy and spacious backpack. It has a main compartment, several pockets, and adjustable straps. The bag is made of durable material, and it comes in a classic navy blue color with vibrant accents. The brand logo is prominently displayed on the front pocket.

**Description:**

The main compartment of my school bag is roomy enough to accommodate my textbooks, notebooks, and other essential school supplies. It has a zipper closure, ensuring the safety of my belongings. There are additional pockets on the front, sides, and inside, allowing me to organize my items efficiently.

The adjustable straps make it easy to carry the bag comfortably on my back. The back panel is padded, providing extra support. The bag's design is not only functional but also stylish, reflecting my personal taste. Overall, my school bag is a reliable companion for my daily academic journey, combining practicality with a touch of individuality.
