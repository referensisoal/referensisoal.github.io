40. buatkan percakapan bahasa inggris yang mencakup correlative conjunction (3 orang) ​


Writing Dialog Using Correlative Conjunctions

Jawaban

Pendahuluan

Sebelum membuat dialog mari pahami terlebih dahulu apa pengertiannya. Correlative conjunctions adalah kata hubung seperti and, but, or dan nor yang digunakan berpasangan dengan both, not only, either, dan neither. Beberapa contohnya:

- both me and my sister hate chicken

- not only my mother but also my father love sports

- I want either coffee or tea

- I want neither coffee nor tea

- We will have both roasted chicken and fried chicken

- I didn’t know whether you’d want to take a walk or ride a bicycle

Pembahasan

Kamil: Hey, Martin! What are you up to?

Martin: Hey Kamil. Hmmm I’m not sure what shoes should I bring tomorrow for camping.

Kamil: What’s the problem? I see that you have many shoes.

Martin: Yeah, but here is the problem. Both me and my brother are going to camping tomorrow. Because we share the same size, he would like to take my most comfortable shoes.

Kamil: Ah I see. I think this problem is not only yours but also mine when I was a kid with my older brother.

Martin: Really, how?

Kamil: Yeah, my brother wanted only either my shoes or a new one. He didn’t want to use his shoes. I don’t understand why.

Martin: Apparently both of our brothers are having a little bit weird personality, huh?

Kamil: Haha yeah I agree. By the way let’s go for a food, we will think about how to fix your problem later. Okay.

Martin: Good idea!
