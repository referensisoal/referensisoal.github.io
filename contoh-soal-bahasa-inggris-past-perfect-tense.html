Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. When I faced behind I saw a shadow and went into a coma for two whole days. When I woke up I realized that shadow was my own shadow.

16. Contoh puisi bahasa inggris menggunakan present perfect dan past tense


ANTHOLOGY OF AMERICAN POETRY

Before flash drives and laptops,

There was the Anthology of American Poetry,

All the verse a spindly armed English major

Could be expected to carry.

In order to make so much genius portable,

The poems were printed on paper so thin

It was translucent;

Hold the poem in question up to the light,

And the one on the next page was visible in reverse.

It didn’t take much skill to translate the next poem

Simultaneously as you read the first;

As in a pair of poems by a Mid-Atlantic author.

In the first a husband instructs his wife of sixty-five years

In which suit and shirt to bury him,

Which hymns he wants sung,

Which prayers are to be read,

And then makes her promise to go on living for his sake;

While on the back of the same sheet,

His other sample,

Has a GI in New Guinea,

Writing to his girl in Pennsylvania,

Proposing marriage,

And how, if he survives,

He will love her longer than forever.

Ron Yazinski

was=past tense

BIRTHING DREAMS

My mother says I did not want to be born.

In last night's dream, I was giving birth,

crying out, pulling in breath. My babies

are adults now. They have long since

knitted themselves into what they would be.

In another dream, the way to the fruit market

is through a narrow passage: stairs

and yellow-painted bricks. The boy

ahead of me slips through the narrow

opening. I have to turn back.

My son, three years old, said: I remember

before I was born. I could feel you walking,

walking. Dark in there!

It is always dark in there. The black

passages of mind or womb will deliver

themselves of whatever resides inside,

stillborn or full of breath.

Which is the greater miracle,

the child or the words? The wonder

of deep desire, woven into the fabric

of speech? The small peach-fuzz head,

still blind, that opens its mouth in protest

against that first inrush of air?

Ruth Cassel Hoffman

24. Contoh Cerita kerumah nenek dalam bahasa inggris menggunakan rumus simple past tense dan Present perfect tense.


Simple Past Tense and Present Perfect Tense

Simple Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk kegiatan yang sudah diselesaikan pada waktu lampau. Kejadian tersebut bisa saja baru terjadi atau terjadi pada beberapa waktu lalu dan sudah selesai.

Bentuk Umum dari Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2

(-) Subject + did not + infinitive without to

(?) Did + Subject + infinitive without to

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense:

(+) Andi went to the park yesterday.

(-) Andi didn't go to the park yesterday.

(?) Did Andi go to the park yesterday?

Present Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kejadian di masa lampau dan efeknya masih dirasakan sampai saat ini. Atau kejadian yang dimulai di masa lampau dan akan diselesaikan di masa yang akan datang.

Bentuk umum dari present perfect tense adalah:

S + Have/has + Past Participle (Verb-3)

Contoh dalam kalimat:

I have been to Australia.She hasn't finished her homework.They have been married for twenty years.I have received the invitation.My sister has had this interesting reading material since she was a teenager.Pembahasan

Yesterday, I went to my grandmother's house. She lives in the countryside.
