30. Percakapan bahasa inggris untuk 6 orang di kelas, secara singkat. Tapi tiap orang 3 kalimat saja.


Make a dialogue between 6 people in the class

Untuk membuat dialog, hal pertama yang harus kita tentukan adalah tema dialog tersebut, yaitu tentang apa yang mereka bicarakan, menentukan berapa orang yang terlibat dalam dialog (dalam hal ini sudah ditentukan 6 orang)

Pembahasan

Situasi : Andara, Anton, Max, Bay, Baity dan Welly sedang melakukan rapat di kelas mengenai field trip kelas mereka. Dalam rapat itu mereka perlu memutuskan tempat dan waktu field trip dan juga membentuk tim untuk field trip.

Andara : Let's start our informal meeting today, we are going to discuss our field trip. Anton, can you remind us about the last meeting?

Anton : Sure, last meeting we have decided to go to Lembang. But we haven't decided about the time.

Andara : Thanks, Anton. Any opinion?

Anton : No worries, Andara.

Max : I think June after the final test is the best time for the field trip.

Bay : Yes, I agree with Max, June is the best time.

Andara : Anyone else agrees to do the field trip on June?

Baity : Yes, agree with the field trip on June after the final test, if I check the schedule the final test will be finished on 15 June, so maybe the 20 June? It's on the weekend by the way.

Max : Yes, that's even better.

Andara : Ok, we decided the field trip on 20 June.

Max : Maybe we can visit the Bosscha observatory, the floating market and some educational places.

Welly : That's a good idea, maybe we can include those places on our field trip lists.

Bay : Yes, and don't forget the farmhouse, I really want to see the Hobbit house!

Anton : Maybe Welly and Bay can help us to make the schedule. We also need someone to help with the accommodation booking.

Welly : Sure, Anton, we'll do our best!

Baity : I can help with that but I need someone to help me.

Anton : Who can team up with Baity?

Bay : I will help to make the schedule, maybe someone else?

Max : I can help you, Baity!

Baity : Oh thanks Max, we'll make a good team!

Welly : if someone else has a recommendation they can talk to us.

Andara : Yes, sure. If anyone has an idea about places to visit they can talk to Bay or Welly.

Bay : Yeah, sure.

Andara : OK, I think that's all for our meeting today. Next meeting, would be on the next two weeks. And I hope everyone ready with their presentation.
