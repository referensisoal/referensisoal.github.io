3. buatlah contoh soal cause dan effect 10 soal beserta jawabannya​


PENJELASAN :

Sure, here are those cause and effect questions and their answers translated into English:

1. Question: What causes acid rain and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: Acid rain is caused by the emission of pollutant gases such as sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides into the atmosphere that react with rainwater. As a result, acid rain can damage soil, water, and plants, and can negatively impact human and animal health.

2. Question: What causes global warming and what is its impact on the world's climate?

Answer: Global warming is caused by an increase in greenhouse gas emissions such as carbon dioxide. The result is an increase in the Earth's average temperature, leading to climate changes like increased intensity of extreme weather and rising sea levels.

3. Question: What causes deforestation and what is its impact on biodiversity?

Answer: Deforestation is often caused by the clearing of forests for agriculture, plantations, or mining. The result is that deforestation can reduce the natural habitats of forest species, threatening biodiversity.

4. Question: What causes forest fires and what is their impact on air quality?

Answer: Forest fires are often caused by human activities, such as logging and land clearing by burning. The result is that forest fires can produce smoke and particles that damage air quality and impact human health.

5. Question: What causes water pollution and what is its impact on marine life?

Answer: Water pollution is often caused by the disposal of industrial or household waste into water. The result is that water pollution can damage water ecosystems, reduce water quality, and negatively impact marine life.

6. Question: What causes obesity and what is its impact on health?

Answer: Obesity is usually caused by unhealthy eating habits and lack of physical activity. The result is that obesity can increase the risk of various diseases, such as diabetes, heart disease, and certain types of cancer.

7. Question: What causes the increase in the use of renewable energy and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: The increase in the use of renewable energy is often due to concerns about climate change and the depletion of fossil fuel sources. The result is that the use of renewable energy can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and has a lower environmental impact compared to fossil energy.

8. Question: What causes the decline in bee populations and what is its impact on agriculture?

Answer: The decline in bee populations is often caused by the use of pesticides, climate change, and loss of habitat. The result is that a decline in bee populations can reduce pollination, which is important for agriculture and food production.

9. Question: What causes the increase in the use of digital technology and what is its impact on society?

Answer: The increase in the use of digital technology is often due to technological advancements and the need to increase efficiency and productivity. The result is that the use of digital technology can affect the way we work, learn, and interact with others.

10. Question: What causes stress and what is its impact on mental health?

Answer: Stress can be caused by various factors, such as work pressure, financial problems, or personal issues. The result is that stress can affect mental and physical health, including an increased risk of depression, anxiety, and heart disease.

11. Contoh dialog tentang cause effect sebab akibat


Contoh Dialog Cause and Effect

Andi: Hi Rudi, are you OK? You look so pale.

Rudi: I am not feeling well because I didn't have breakfast this morning. I have a tummy ache.

Andi: Here is some bread. You can have it. Did you wake up late?

Rudi: Yes, I slept late last night so I woke up late and didn't have time for breakfast. Anyway, thanks for the bread. I am feeling much better now.

Andi: No worries. I am happy to help.

Pembahasan

Cause and Effect (Causative) adalah hubungan antara dua hal atau kejadian, dimana hal yang satu menyebabkan terjadinya hal yang lainnya.

CAUSE ⇒ the reason for something happening

EFFECT ⇒ what happened

CAUSE and EFFECT ⇒ explain why something happened

Dalam bahasa Inggris, kata-kata yang sering digunakan untuk menunjukkan adanya cause and effect, diantaranya  because, since, due to, as, so,  dan therefore.

Kata-kata tersebut merupakan subordinate conjunction, yaitu kata-kata yang menghubungkan antara main clause dan subordinate clause. Berikut contoh kalimat cause and effect:

I came late to school this morning because I woke up late.It was raining so I just stayed at home.He turns on the heater since it is cold outside.He turns on the air conditioner as it is getting hot inside.

Lana and Ben are classmates. They are having a conversation on Monday morning before the flag ceremony in their school yard.

Cause and Effect Relation

Cause secara sederhana diartikan sebagai alasan atau motif seseorang melakukan sebuah tindakan. Dalam kata lain, cause adalah alasan mengapa sesuatu terjadi. Effect diartikan hasil atau akibat dari sebuah tindakan. Contohnya:

It rained very hard so I got wet.

Cause = It rained.

Effect = I got wet.

EXPLANATION

Cause and effect, kalimat yang terhubung adanya keterangan sebab akibat dengan bantuan frasa, seperti :

Due to, because of, because, owing to, therefore, since, as, for, as the result dan sebagainya.

Dalam penulisan frasa cause and effect diatas terdapat perbedaan. Frasa-frasa diatas pada umumnya tertulis dengan diikuti oleh kata kerja ataupun kata benda.

Berikut ini signal phrase yang harus diikuti dengan noun/noun phrase :

Because of, owing to, due to, as a result of

Pattern :

Signal phrase + noun/noun phrase

Exp 

The students don't come to school because of the coronavirus impact

↪Signal phrase : because

↪Cause : the coronavirus impact

↪Effect : The students don't come to school

Berikut ini signal phrase yang harus diikuti dengan clause/verb phrase :

Because, since, as, for

Pattern :

Signal phrase+S+verb

Exp:

He stayed in the hospital several weeks for he was infected by the coronavirus

↪Signal Phrase  : for

↪Cause : he was infected by the coronavirus

↪Effect  : stayed in the hospital several weeks

33. Dialog cause and effect tentang covid 19


Jawaban:It's a dialogue between a mom and her little daughter:

Daughter: Mom, why does everyone wearing a mask now?

Mom: Because of the coronavirus outbreak everyone must wearing a mask. It would help us from catching the virus.

Daughter: Is coronavirus dangerous, Mom? My teacher said we should stay at home and learning from home too.

Mom: Yes, It's dangerous and contagious, so everyone should follow the rule, wash your hands regularly, social distancing, wearing a mask when you go out, stay at home and only go outside when necessary.

Daughter: Yes, that's what my teacher has said too.

Penjelasan:Hubungan antara cause and effect dalam bahasa Indonesia sehari-hari disebut hubungan sebab akibat.

'A cause' memiliki hubungan yang tidak bisa dipisahkan dengan 'an effect.' Kita tidak bisa menyebutkan bahwa suatu kalimat adalah 'a cause' jika kalimat tersebut tidak memiliki 'an effect'.

Secara singkat definisi masing-masing sebagai berikut:

CAUSE adalah alasan sesuatu hal bisa terjadi (the reason for something happening)

EFFECT adalah what happened (apa yang terjadi)

CAUSE & EFFECT adalah menjelaskan mengapa sesuatu terjadi

Untuk menghubungkan cause dan effect digunakan signal words, diantaranya:

due to

therefore

because

because of

as

so

CONTOH KALIMAT CAUSE AND EFFECT:

1. Because of his illness, Barry has resigned from his job

CAUSE pada kalimat diatas adalah his illness

EFFECT-nya adalah Barry has resigned from his job

Signal word yang digunakan adalah because of (diikuti noun)

2. Andi didn't pass the exam because he didn't study well.

CAUSE pada kalimat diatas adalah didn't study well

EFFECT-nya adalah Andi didn't pass the exam.

Signal word yang digunakan adalah because (diikuti oleh verb)

3. I slept late last night so I am sleepy now.

CAUSE pada kalimat diatas adalah sleep late

EFFECT-nya adalah I am sleepy now.

Signal word: so
