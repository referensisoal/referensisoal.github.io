PQ = q - p = (7  -x    3) - (5   x   2) = (2    -2x    1)
|PQ| = √(2² + (-2x)² + 1²)
⇒ 3 = √(5 + 4x²)
⇒ 9 = 5 + 4x²
⇒ 4 = 4x²
⇒ x² = 1
⇒ x = 1

30. Tolong Jawab Soal Bahasa Inggris Peminatan Saya Ini Kak Susah Kali Jawabnya Kak (╥﹏╥)


EXPRESSIONS

1. (C) Why don't you have more intellectual exercises

2. (D) How about join their activities

3. (H) Why don't you read English books especially dealing with tests

4. (A) One thing you could do is go to fitness centers

5. (E) May be you could try to enjoy every activity

6. (G) It might be a good idea to plan fun activities to look forward to every week.

7. (B) It might be a good idea to avoid food with calories.

8. (F) It might be a good idea to be a workaholic.

ELABORATION

Perintah soal yang diminta adalah terkait pencocokkan saran yang sesuai dari setiap masalah yang dihadapi dengan menggunakan ungkapan saran.

Problems & Suggestions

1. How can I improve my logic ability?

(C) Why don't you have more intellectual exercises

2. How can I get along with my classmate better?

(D) How about join their activities

3. How can I get a good TOEFL score?

(H) Why don't you read English books especially dealing with tests

4. What can I do to have a well-built body?

(A) One thing you could do is go to fitness centers

5. What should I do to be more self-confident?

(E) May be you could try to enjoy every activity

6. What could help me be more cheerful?

(G) It might be a good idea to plan fun activities to look forward to every week.

7. How can I become slimmer?

(B) It might be a good idea to avoid food with calories.

8. What can do to get more money?

(F) It might be a good idea to be a workaholic.

Berikut terjemahannya dari setiap masalah dan sarannya:

C. Perbanyaklah latihan mengasah intelektual

D. Bergabunglah/ambilah bagian dari semua kegiatan mereka.

H. membaca buku bahasa Inggris terutama yang berhubungan dengan tes.

4. Apa yang dapat saya lakukan untuk memiliki tubuh yang tegap?

A. Pergi ke tempat fitnes.

5. Apa yang harus saya lakukan agar lebih percaya diri?

E. Cobalah menikmati setiap kegiatan yang ada.

6. Apa yang bisa membantu saya menjadi lebih ceria?

G. Rencanakanlah kegiatan yang menyenangkan di kemudian hari setiap minggunya.

B. hindari makanan berkalori
