7. MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRISMATERI : APPLICATION LETTER KELAS : XII SMA____________________TOLONG DIBANTUKETERANGAN : • PERTANYAAN ADA DILAMPIRAN ATAS !• KALAU MENJAWAB HARUS DISERTAKAN DENGAN PENJELASAN ! [ TERIMA KASIH ]​


APPLICATION LETTER

1) The applicant's address

2) Date

3) The recipient's contact information

4) Salutation

5) Introduction

6) Main body and relevant work experience

7) Conclusion

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

EXPLANATION

A job application letter is one of business letters, contains correspondence between an applicant and organization or companies that publish a job vacancy.

Generic structure

1) The applicant's address

It contains the applicant's contact information. It is placed on the left hand-side of the paper, towards the top.

2) Date

It contains the date when the applicant writes a letter.

3) The recipient's contact information

It contains the recipient's information (name of the responsible officer, title of the responsible officer, name and address of the organization).

4) Salutation

It contains regards (words of greeting) to the recipient. The salutation in a business letter (a job application lettee) is always formal.

For example:

Dear Mr. Paterson (if the contact is a man's name)

Dear Mrs. Dew (if the contact is a woman's name)

5) Introduction

It contains statement of the post or a job are the applicant is interested in, the reason for making personal-referral, and how the vacancy is known.

6) Main body and relevant work experience

It contains a summary of the applicant's background and critical skills that make him/her qualified for the job position.

7) Conclusion

It contains the applicant's thanking to the person who considerates the application letter, the applicant's expectation, the applicant's availability for the job, and where can be contacted.

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

It contains the warm regards, courteous words to end the letter and the apllicant's signature.

20. Tolong dibantu soal kelas XII SMA tentang himpunan penyelesaian. Tks


2 sin (2x + phi) - V3 = 0
2sin (2x + phi) = V3
sin (2x + phi) = (V3)/2
sin (2x + phi) = 60°
sin (2x + phi) = sin 60°
2x + phi = 60° + k.360
2x = 60 - phi + k.360
2x = -120 + k.360
x = -60 + k.180
untuk k = 0, => x = -60
untuk k = 1, => x = 120
untuk k = 2 => x = 300
HP = { 120°, 300° }
HP = { 2/3phi, 5/3phi }

32. tolong di bantu jawab kelas XII bahasa Inggris jangan asal asalan jawab​


Read the text and answer the questions according to the readings!

A fox fell into a well and couldn't get out. By and by a thirsty goat came along. Seeing the fox in the well it asked if the water was good. "Good," said the fox, "It's the best water I've tasted in all my life. Come down and try it yourself." The goat was thirsty so he got into the well. When he had drunk enough, he looked around but there was no way to get out. Then the fox said, "I have a good idea. You stand on your hind legs and put your forelegs against the side of the well. Then I'll climb on your back, from there. l'll step on your horns, and I can get out. And when I'm out, I'll help you out of the well. The goat did as he was asked and the fox got on his back and climbed out of the well. Then he coolly walked away. The goat called out loudly after him and reminded him of his promise to help him out. The fox merely turn to him and said, "if you only had thought carefully about getting out, you wouldn't have jumped into the well." The goat felt very sad. He called out loudly. An old man walking nearby heard him and put a plank into the well. The goat out and thanked the old man.

PEMBAHASAN

1. The text tells the story of a fox and a goat.

➡ Teks tersebut menceritakan kisah seekor rubah dan seekor kambing.

Jawaban: A fox and a goatKeterangan: Karena tokoh utama dalam teks tersebut adalah rubah dan kambing.
