6. bahasa Inggris : change the following sentences into interrogatives 1. my mother Made a cake for me yesterday2. he write a letter for bus grandparent yesterday 3. they came to my party yesterday ​


+ My mother made a cake for me yesterday
? Did my mother make a cake for me yesterday?

+ He wrote a letter for busy grandparent yesterday
? Did he write a letter for busy grandparent yesterday?

+ They came to my party yesterday
? Did they come to my party yesterday?

Penjelasan : Bentuk kalimat - kalimat diatas diubah tenses nya dari Positive Simple Past Tense menjadi Interrogative Simple Past Tense. Dengan menambahkan kata “Did” di depan Subjek, mengubah Kata kerja V2 menjadi V1, dan menambahkan tanda tanya “?” Di akhir kalimat.

11. Azriel and Shinta.......a cake for the party now(make)​


Jawab: are making (sedang membuat)

= Azriel and Shinta are making a cake for the party now

(Azriel dan Shinta sedang membuat kue utk pesta Sekarang)

Catatan:

Karena ada keterangan waktu “now” (sekarang) maka tenses yg digunakan adalah Present Continues:

(+) S + to be (is/am/are) + Verb+ing + O

= Azriel and Shinta are making a cake for the party now

* menggunakan to be (are) karena subject jamak (Azriel and Shinta)

=============================

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Present Continues

Kode Mapel: 5

32. kalimat pasif dari "he chose the blue tie for the party"​


Jawaban :

Present Past Tense

Active Voice ( S + V2 + O)

= He chose the blue tie for the party.

Change Into Passive Voice
Passive Voice
( O + to be (was,were) + V3 + S )

= The blue tie was chosen by him for the party.
