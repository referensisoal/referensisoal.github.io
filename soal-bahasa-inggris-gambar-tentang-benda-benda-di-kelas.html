20. sebutkan lah 10 benda benda yang ada di kelasmu beserta kalimatnya dalam bahasa inggris!!​


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh 10 benda yang mungkin ada di dalam kelas beserta kalimatnya dalam bahasa Inggris:

1. Desk (Meja): The desk in my classroom is made of wood.

2. Chair (Kursi): Please find an empty chair and have a seat.

3. Whiteboard (Papan tulis): The teacher writes important notes on the whiteboard.

4. Bookshelf (Rak buku): The bookshelf is filled with various textbooks and novels.

5. Globe (Globe): The globe on the teacher's desk helps us learn about different countries.

6. Clock (Jam): The clock on the wall shows that it's time for the next class.

7. Computer (Komputer): We use computers in the classroom for research and assignments.

8. Backpack (Ransel): Please keep your backpack under your desk during the class.

9. Pencil (Pensil): Can I borrow a pencil from you? I forgot mine.

10. Window (Jendela): The sunlight enters the classroom through the window.
