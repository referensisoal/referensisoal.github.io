8. Bagaimana cara menggunakan were / was / did ?


was, were, did digunakan utk past tense.

was = utk subject tunggal (i, she, he, it, name)
were = subject jamak (you, they, we)

was dan were digunakan utk verbal past tense (kalimat tdk membutuhkan kalimat kerja)
contoh : i was here two hours ago.

was dan were juga digunakan utk past progressive dan passive past tense:
contoh : we were playing at the yard when i came.

passive : this book was bought by me.

did digunakan utk verbal past tense, pd kalimat tanya dan negatif
contoh: he bought a new book.
he did not buy a new book.
did he buy a new book?Were dan was untuk kalimat nominal
Were untuk you, they, we.
Cth:
You were happy.
They were students from this school.
We were sad about the news.

Was untuk I, she, he, it
Cth: 
I was happy.
She was a teacher.
He was angry at me.
Viko was surprised.

Did untuk kalimat verbal buat semua subjek
Cth:
I did my homework last night.
They did their work yesterday.
She did it.
He did well at the exam.

Penjelasan:

Past tense

Adalah kegiatan atau kehidupan kita diwaktu lampau maupun sudah berlalu.

Rumus :

(+) Subject + verb 2

(-) Subject + didn't + verb 1

(?) Subject + did + verb 1

Contoh :

Sherly (study) math

(+) Sherly studied math

(-) Sherly didn't study math

(?) Did Sherly study math ?

Untuk soal ini, maka kata yang digunakan adalah did karena diketahui waktunya sudah berlalu.

19. Apa perbedaan to be and do did was were


To be was dan were digunakan utk peristiwa yg sudah terjadi dan subjectnya diikuti kata sifat

Contoh :
He was angry because you were late.

Did digunakan utk peristiwa yg sudah terjadi, subjectnya diikuti verb, dan bentuk kalimat negatif atau kalimat tanya.

Contoh :
She didn't want to study English yesterday.

20. Apa bedanya was/were dalam bahasa inggris?


Was :
1. Verb/Kata kerja ke-2 dan ke-3 dari is
2. Untuk objek tunggal/singular
3. Untuk He, She, It, I

Were :
1. Verb/Kata kerja ke-2 dan ke-3 dari are
2. Untuk objek yg lebih dari satu/plural
3. Untuk I, You, We, They

P.S. : "I" bisa menggunakan was/were. Tergantung struktur kalimatnya.was utk I, she,he,dan it (tunggal), sedangkan were utk
you, they, we (jamak).

25. buatlah pertanyaan dari kata HAD,WAS,WERE dan DID masing masing 5 pertanyaan menggunakan bahasa Inggris berserta artinya. ​


Jawaban:

HAD (Telahkah/Pernahkah)

1. Had she visited that museum before? (Apakah dia pernah mengunjungi museum itu sebelumnya?)

2. Had they finished their homework by the time you arrived? (Apakah mereka sudah menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumah mereka saat kamu tiba?)

3. Had he ever traveled to America? (Apakah dia pernah pergi ke Amerika?)

4. Had you met Ilham before he moved to the city? (Apakah kamu pernah bertemu dengan Ilham sebelum dia pindah ke kota?)

WAS (Apakah)

1. Was she at the party last night? (Apakah dia ada di pesta semalam?)

2. Was it raining when you left the house? (Apakah hujan ketika kamu meninggalkan rumah?)

3. Was he feeling sick yesterday? (Apakah dia merasa sakit kemarin?)

4. Was the movie good? (Apakah filmnya bagus?)

5. Was the restaurant crowded when you arrived? (Apakah restorannya ramai saat kamu tiba?)

Were (Apakah (Bentuk Jamak)):

1. Were they at the game yesterday? (Apakah mereka ada di pertandingan kemarin?)

2. Were you and Sarah classmates in college? (Apakah kamu dan Sarah teman sekelas di perguruan tinggi?)

3. Were the children playing in the park this morning? (Apakah anak-anak bermain di taman pagi ini?)

4. Were the students excited about the field trip? (Apakah para siswa senang dengan perjalanan lapangan itu?)

5. Were there any problems with the computer system? (Apakah ada masalah dengan sistem komputer?)

Did (Melakukan/Apa):

1. Did you finish your homework last night? (Apakah kamu menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumahmu semalam?)

2. Did they call you this morning? (Apakah mereka meneleponmu pagi ini?)

3. Did she enjoy the concert? (Apakah dia menikmati konsernya?)

4. Did he eat lunch at the cafeteria? (Apakah dia makan siang di kafetaria?)

5. Did you watch the news on TV yesterday? (Apakah kamu menonton berita di TV kemarin?)

Hope this helps, buat jawaban terverifikasi :)

26. The teacher was reading newspaper while the students_______ the assignment.a. didb. doc. will dod. have donee. were doing


Untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas, jawabannya adalah..

Answer :

The teacher was reading newspaper while the students were doing the assignment.

Pembahasan

Karena tanda waktu yang digunakan pada kalimat tersebut adalah "while", maka kalimat nya termasuk ke dalam past continuous tense. While merupakan tanda waktu yang berfungsi untuk menghubungkan dua kejadian/perbuatan yang kejadiannya terjadi pada waktu yang sama.

Untuk penggunaan "while" dalam kalimat ada 2 :

Main clause nya simple past, time clause nya past continuous. Contoh : The teacher read newspaper while the students were doing the assignment.Jika main clause nya past continuous, time clause nya juga past continuous. Contoh : The teacher was reading newspaper while the students were doing the assignment.
