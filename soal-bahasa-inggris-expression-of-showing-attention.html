Jawaban dicetak tebal pilihan C. Checking Understanding karena pada saat seseorang menjelaskan suatu hal yang penting dan cukup panjang kepada orang lain, maka orang yang memberikan penjelasan tersebut harus memastikan bahwa orang yang mendengarkan penjelasannya masih fokus mendengarkan dan memahaminya.

33. penjekasan tentang expression of showing and giving attention


Answer:
________
Giving attention is our attitude in responding to our interlocutor (this is a simple meaning)
Example:
=> I know what you mean, Elsa.

Showing attention is a common phrase we use to pay attention to others around us.
Example:
=> I heard your beloved cat died yesterday, I'm sorry to hear that.

37. Dialog yang ada asking repetition,admiration,showing attention,expression of certainty and uncertainty


Joko : “Hafid, the reason why I called you to my office is to congratulate you on becoming the new production                       manager.”

Hafid : “pardon me, Mr. Joko. What did you just say ? Would you mind repeating that again!”

Joko : “ I said ‘congratulations on becoming the new production manager” I have been impressed with your                         work.

Hafid : “Oh my God, thank you Mr Joko. I’m sure I can do this job well.”

Joko : “ I hope so.” 

The bold type in the dialogue above are the example of how to express asking for repetition and the italic is the example of how to express repeating.
