13. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

15. buatkan dialog bahasa Inggris simple present continuous tense dengan tema kegiatan di dalam kelas


Present Continuous Tense

Present Continuous Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian sebagai berikut:

1. Situasi dimana suatu kejadian tengah berlangsung sekarang.

Contoh : Mr. Tony is making design of the house.


2. Situasi dimana suatu kejadian sudah di rencanakan dengan pasti untuk terjadi di masa depan.

Contoh : The class is going to start at 8 AM tomorrow


3. Situasi dimana sebuah perubahan atau perkembangan sedang berlangsung untuk waktu yang lama.

Contoh : My parents is getting older.


Pattern atau formula untuk present continuous tense adalah:

(+) S + is/am/are + V ing

(-) S + is/am/are not + Ving

(?) is/ am/ are + S + Ving?

NB:

is untuk subject He, She, It (singular)

are untuk subject you, we, they (plural)

am untuk subject I.

Pembahasan

Dialog Bahasa Inggris menggunakan present continuous tense dengan tema kegiatan dalam kelas:

Annie: Hello Anton, what are you doing?

Anton: I am reading about Sangkuriang story. Mrs. Santi asked us to read about Sangkuriang yesterday.

Annie: Really? Noone told me about it.

Anton: Are you absence yesterday?

Annie: Yes, I got influenza yesterday.

Anton: You may start to read now Annie. Mrs. Santi will come at 11.

Annie: Yes. I am starting to read the story right now.

Anton: Good luck!

Simple present tenses dalam teks

1. About elephants

Elephants are the heaviest land animals.They are also intelligent and have good memories.Most of them live in Afrika and Asia.They use in Lampung, Indonesia.They use their long trunks almost like an arm.They eat grass and plants.

2. About tigers

Tigers are the biggest of all cats.They live in grasslands and forests.Their stripped coat gives them good camouflage when they hunt.They love meat to eat.

3. About bears

Bear have thick four coats ...Most of them live in northern parts of the world. They are large and powerful. They have a good sense of smell. Some of them eat meat and some of them eat honey. Some bears find a snug place to hibernate.

4. About monkeys

Monkeys are clever mammals ...They live in groups called troops. Monkey eats plants, birds, eggs, small animals, and insects.Most of them live in the forest.

Simple present tense merupakan tenses sederhana yang menandakan peristiwa dilakukan waktu sekarang atau kenyataan.

Pembahasan

Simple Present Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan, kebenaran umum, rutinitas, kenyataan, atau pendapat.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: often, usually, always, sometimes, rarely, seldom, every day, twice a week, nowadays.

Bentuk simple present terbagi menjadi dua, yaitu nominal sentence dan verbal sentence.

Pola nominal sentence: S + is/am/are + OPola verbal sentence: S + verb base or + s/s + O

Example:

She is a doctor. (nominal sentence)Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius.

Person 2: Yes, I have already handed it in to the boss.

Pola/Rumus :

Nominal Sentence :

(+) S + Tobe (am/is/are) + A/N/A

(-) S + Tobe (am/is/are + Not + A/N/A

(?) Tobe (am/is/are) + S + A/N/A

is = she, he, it, singular noun/subject

are = you, they, we, plural noun/subject

Verbal Sentence :

- Subject : I, You, They, We, Plural Noun/Subject

(+) S + V 1 + Complement

(-) S + do not (don't) + V 1 + Complement

(?) Do + S + V1 + Complement

-Subject : She, He, It, Singular Noun/Subject

(+) S + V 1 s/es/ies + Complement

(-) S + Doesn't (does not) + V 1 + Complement
