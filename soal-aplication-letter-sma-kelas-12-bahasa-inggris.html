The following is an example of a letter of application sent with a resume to apply for a job. Use this example as a guideline when writing your own job application cover letters. Also see below for an example a job application letter sent by email and tips for what to include and how to write a letter to apply for a job.

Your job application letter is an opportunity to highlight your most relevant qualifications and experiences, enhancing your resume, and increasing your chances of being called for an interview.

 

Your letter should detail your specific qualifications for the position and the skills you would bring to the employer. See below for how to email your application letter.

What to Include in Your Letter

As with all cover letters, the body of this job application letter is divided into three sections: the introduction, which details why the applicant is writing; the body, which discusses relevant qualifications; and the closing, which thanks the reader and provides contact information and follow-up details.

5. MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRISMATERI : APPLICATION LETTER KELAS : XII SMA____________________TOLONG DIBANTUKETERANGAN : • PERTANYAAN ADA DILAMPIRAN ATAS !• KALAU MENJAWAB HARUS DISERTAKAN DENGAN PENJELASAN ! [ TERIMA KASIH ]​


APPLICATION LETTER

1) The applicant's address

2) Date

3) The recipient's contact information

4) Salutation

5) Introduction

6) Main body and relevant work experience

7) Conclusion

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

EXPLANATION

A job application letter is one of business letters, contains correspondence between an applicant and organization or companies that publish a job vacancy.

Generic structure

1) The applicant's address

It contains the applicant's contact information. It is placed on the left hand-side of the paper, towards the top.

2) Date

It contains the date when the applicant writes a letter.

3) The recipient's contact information

It contains the recipient's information (name of the responsible officer, title of the responsible officer, name and address of the organization).

4) Salutation

It contains regards (words of greeting) to the recipient. The salutation in a business letter (a job application lettee) is always formal.

For example:

Dear Mr. Paterson (if the contact is a man's name)

Dear Mrs. Dew (if the contact is a woman's name)

5) Introduction

It contains statement of the post or a job are the applicant is interested in, the reason for making personal-referral, and how the vacancy is known.

6) Main body and relevant work experience

It contains a summary of the applicant's background and critical skills that make him/her qualified for the job position.

7) Conclusion

It contains the applicant's thanking to the person who considerates the application letter, the applicant's expectation, the applicant's availability for the job, and where can be contacted.

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

It contains the warm regards, courteous words to end the letter and the apllicant's signature.

6. write the parts of aplication letter and explain them​


PARTS OF APPLICATION LETTER

Sebelum menulis surat lamaran sebaiknya kita tahu beberapa poin yang harus ada dalam penulisan surat lamaran. Secara umum, struktur surat lamaran adalah sebagai berikut:

Date. Tanggal yang ditulis adalah tanggal penulisan surat.

Contact detail. Bagian ini berisi nama, alamat, nomor telepon dan alamat email Anda sebagai penulis surat lamaran. Gunakan alamat email yang formal dan profesional.

Address adalah alamat surat yang dituju. Tuliskan nama dan alamat perusahaan.

Salutation atau salam. Merupakan bagian dimana Anda menuliskan nama penerima surat. Sebaiknya yang dituliskan adalah nama penerima surat bukan hanya jabatan dari penerima surat misalnya, HRD Manager

Body of the letter adalah bagian utama dari surat lamaran, beberapa point yang harus ada yaitu posisi yang dilamar, jelaskan mengenai kualifikasi, skills dan pencapaian yang pernah . Alasan mereka harus memilih Anda.

Closing. Untuk mengakhiri surat lamaran sebaiknya menggunakan kalimat yang formal seperti "sincerely atau Yours truly"

Signature adalah bagian dimana Anda menuliskan nama lengkap dan tanda-tangan. Umumnya jika menggunakan email, hanya menuliskan nama lengkap.

All job seekers should understand that a cover letter is comprised of several parts, including:

Contact details.(rincian kontak)Salutation.(salam)The main body.(tubuh utama)A strong closing.(penutupan yang kuat)Signature.(tanda tangan)

There are eight parts of a business letter:

1. The Heading

This part includes the name of the sender and their address.

2. Date

The date the letter is written down is listed in this section.

3. Recipient's address

This is the section where the recipient and their address is being listen.

4. Salutation

This part is also often called as greetings. It's always formal.

The body is divided in three parts:

5. Opening

Introduction to the topic and expressing the purpose of writing the letter.

6. Main

Discussing the topic.

7. Closing

Finishing the topic and shows gratitude for the attention the recipient has given.

8. Signature line

The signature of the sender.
