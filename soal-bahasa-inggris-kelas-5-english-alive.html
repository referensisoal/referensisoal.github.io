Jawaban:

Stay Alive is an underground game that invites players to explore a large mansion that contains many spirits with a leader in the form of a woman in a red dress. The surprising thing about this game is that if a player dies in the game world then the same thing will happen in the real world

Another "sickening" thing is that this game is unstoppable (pause) which makes players have to finish the game before they experience game over. Then began the struggle of some horror movie stereotype characters in revealing the secrets in this game

Jumpscare which is the mainstay weapon for horror films appears so unexpectedly in this film and is very short so that it almost doesn't give the audience the impression of "shock" and can even make you dumbfounded, not to mention that some sadistic scenes appear very short (a fraction of a second) or takes place in a dark environment so it is quite difficult to see what is happening to some of the characters.

The positive side is the appearance of the game which looks original, moreover, to start this game the player must cast a spell on the screen, the way this film unites the game world with the real world is only seen towards the end of this film and for me it can still be considered

unique and cool.

The next compliment lies in the development of the antagonist character Elizabeth Bathory, who although appears very one-dimensional and not 100% according to the original history, but is able to describe the cruelty of the Blood Countess very well, especially the design of the torture chamber with a bathtub which further strengthens Bathory's ambition to stay young.

Considered the most active killer of his time according to the Guinness World Records, Elizabeth Bathory became one of the inspirations for the fictional character "Dracula" even though his prestige was inferior to Vlad The Impaler.

Born and raised among the "Bathory" nobles who are said to like torturing people, the number of victims of Elizabeth Bathory is estimated at > 600 people, most of whom are small children (especially virgin women), but the most terrible thing is that she tortures her victims a lot for the sake of pleasure. sheer and purportedly involved in cannibalism and black magic.

One of her most famous acts is the act of bathing in virgin blood in order to maintain her beauty.

But in the end Elizabeth Bathory was arrested and sentenced to imprisonment in a secluded place because of her family's influence which made Elizabeth Bathory unable to be punished fairly. Elizabeth Bathory died 5 years later and was buried in a mysterious place whose whereabouts were unknown until now.

In the end, this film is not so scary but is able to provide another version of Elizabeth Bathory, besides that the video game part is still interesting to watch and is another strength of this film.
