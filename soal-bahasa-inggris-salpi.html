18. Dekripsi tentang sapi dalam bahasa inggris


kelas : VII
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Descriptive text
kata kunci : Cattle, cow

pembahasan:
Cow is a very famous because we can find it in almost all parts of the world. In India, cows are sacred and are allowed to roam the street free. Cows are known as important domestic animal which are raised as livestock for meat, as variety of nutrious dairy products, and as draft animals (oxen or bullocks that pull carts, plows). An adult male cow is called a bull. An adult female is called a cow. The young is called a calf. A female who has not given birth is called a heifer.

The weight of adult cow varies, depending on the breed. They weigh between 400-1600 pounds. Cows are herbivores, meaning they eat corn, hay, barley, beet pulp, grass, and wheat. They are ruminants, meaning they can digest food that can normally not be digested. That's why they are known for regurgitating and re-chewing their food. They eat 50 pounds of food and drink 35 gallons a water a day. A cow has one stomach with four compartments. They are the rumen, the reticulum, the omasum, and the abomasum.
