23. apa itu simple past dan apa itu continuous? dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban :

- Simple Past -

The simple past tense, sometimes called preterite, is used to talk about an event that was finished at a time before the present. The simple past is the basic form of the past tense in English. Events can be recent in the past or long in the past, and the duration of their occurrence is not important.

- Present Continous Tense -

Present continuous tense is a tense that functions to express an action that is being done in the present (when speaking) and something in the future. The continual present is formed by the auxiliary verb "to be" such as am, is, and is followed by the present participle (V1 + ing).

1. When my father went home, my mother was preparing our dinner.

2. The girl was playing with her dolls when I passed in front of her house.

EXPLANATION

Kita sering menggunakan "Past Continuous" dan "Simple Past" dalam satu kalimat yang sama seperti soal yang ditanyakan di atas. Baik "Simple past" maupun "Past Continuous" menjabarkan kejadian pada masa lampau.

Rumus:

Simple past ➡ S+verb2+ O/C

Past continuous ➡ S+to be(was/were)+Verb-ing+O/C

-

Pada umumnya, kejadian pada "Simple past" itu pendek dan tidak terduga. Kejadian ini menginterupsi kejadian lama yang biasanya menggunakan "Past Continuous."

Contoh:

I was washing the clothes when my friend phoned me.

Induk klausa menggunakan pola "Past Continuous" (Washing the clothes). Ini menandakan kejadiannya berlangsung lebih lama daripada yang menggunakan pola "Simple past" (phoned me). Aksi yang menggunakan "Simple past" pendek dan menginterupsi aksi yang menggunakan "Past continuous."

Ketika dalam satu kalimat terdapat aksi "Simple past" dan "Past continuous" biasanya menggunakan penghubung "When" atau "While."

Penghubung "When" diikuti oleh kalimat bentuk "Simple past." Penghubung "While" diikuti oleh kalimat bentuk "Past Continuous." Selain itu, bisa digunakan untuk dua aksi yang berlangsung bersamaan.

32. buat tensis bahasa inggris simple past, past perfect, past continuous, past perfect continuous dengan kalimat positif, negatif dan introgatif


Contoh kalimat – Past Perfect tense:
They had been here before 1945 (+)
- mereka telah berada disini sebelum tahun 1945
They had not been here before 1945 (-)
- mereka belum berada disini sebelum tahun 1945
Had they been here before 1945 ? (?)
- apakah mereka telah berada disini sebelum 1945 ?

- dia telah pergi ke Amsterdam ketika aku meneleponnya
She had not gone to Amsterdam when I called her (-)

Past continuous tense or also commonly known as past progressive tense is a tense that is used to express activities that are happening at a certain time in the past.
The action / activity has been and is ongoing but has not been completed at that time and sometimes also interspersed with an action / activity that occurs briefly.

as example ;
1.my mother 'was' cooking fried rice while I 'was' repairing my laptop.

2.'were' yo u working all night yesterday?

3.rina 'wasn't' doing her home work when Santi called her.

4.we 'weren't' playing outside when the teacher 'came'.
