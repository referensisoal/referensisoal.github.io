2. pidato bahasa Inggris tentang independent in everyday life​


Jawaban:

Assalamualikum wr. wb.

Peace be upon you, and Allah mercy and blessings.

Greetings to all of us, gratefully endlessly, let us give it to Allah SWT, so, we are always given health and grace in this occasion.

Due all respect to all of the people of the Indonesian country. As well as my friends who are in all corners of the country, future founders of the nation.

In the atmosphere of Indonesia’s independence day, I want to inflame the spirit of Indonesian independence, which seems, it has eroded on the souls of Indonesian youth. We, as the Indonesian people, certainly still remember how great the struggles of our heroes were in fighting for independence from the hands of the colonialists. Their services will be priceless.

As a great nation, we must be able to appreciate the services of our heroes. However, what is more important at this time is how can we fill independence? Filling this independence we can do with various activities in accordance with our respective fields to lead to the ideals of independence which are essentially the ideals of heroes, namely the formation of a just and prosperous social life.

We live in an age as a generation who carry the mandate of freedom fighters to fill independence as well as possible. The role of young people like us is very much needed in order to fight the enemies of the nation and state. We have great potential in bringing this ark of Indonesian leadership to the fore. Then, what kind of struggles can we do as you young? There are many things we can do to fill independence, one of which is by studying hard for the sake of ideals and making the nation proud.

My friends, what our nation is facing today is really hard. In an uncertain domestic political, economic, and social situation, it forces us to take part in guarding the running of the wheels of the nation and state government. Therefore, let us fight together for the nation and state of Indonesia with the things we have, with the things we are able to do, and with all our souls and bodies to serve the nation.

My fellow countrymen and homeland, let us continue the spirit of our heroes with the spirit of development. Because development is also a struggle that is no less important than the struggle for independence. What does it mean to be free if life gets more miserable?

Once again, let’s fill in the commemoration of independence day by increasing the spirit of struggle by realizing the spirit of development to fill independence.

Okay friends, I hope that is a few words that I can say. Hopefully it can make us all as a young generation who are tough and modest in filling Indonesia’s independence. Because actually filling and defending independence is more difficult than winning independence. As said Ir. Soekarno “My struggle is easier because it expels the invaders, but your struggle will be more difficult because it is against your own people”.

I hope that is what I can say, there is something less and less I apologize sincerely.

Finally, let us continue to pray and struggle. Allah is with us, Merdeka !!!!!!!

Wassalamu’alaikum wr.wb.

PRESENT SIMPLE

1. [-] Mother does not cook rice everyday

2. [-] Father does not go to office everyday

3. [-] We do not swim in the river every week

4. [-] They do not eat rice everyday

Explanation

Keempat kalimat di atas termasuk kalimat yg menggunakan simple present tense.

Simple Present/Present Simple adalah verb tense yg digunakan untuk menjabarkan :

~ Habit, aktivitas yg berulang

~ Fakta, kebenaran umum

~ Kegiatan yg sudah terjadwalkan dlm waktu dekat

Time signal :

~ Everyday

~ Always

~ Every week

~ Sometimes

~ Often

~ Once a week

Rumus

Verbal sentence [Predikatnya berupa kata kerja]

[+] S+verb1(s/es)+O/C

[- ] S+do/does+not+verb1+O/C

[?] Do/does+S+verb1+O/C ?

Contoh :

[+] She brushes her teeth everyday

[- ] She does not brush her teeth everyday

[?] Does she brush her teeth everyday ?

Note :

~ Pakai Aux. "do" untuk subyek [I, They, We, You] pd kalimat (-) (?) dan tanpa akhiran -s/es pd kata kerjanya untuk kalimat (+)

~ Pakai Aux "does" untuk subyek [It, She, He, Nama orang] pd kalimat (-) (?) dan menggunakan akhiran -s/es untuk kalimat (+).

Non-Verbal sentence [Predikatnya berupa kopula dan diikuti oleh ANA (Adjective-Noun-Adverb]

[+] S+to be(is/am/are)+A/N/A

[-] S+to be(is/am/are)+NOT+A/N/A

[?] To be(is/am/are)+S+A/N/A ?

Contoh

[+] I am a doctor

[ -] I am not a doctor

[?] Am I a doctor ?
