32. kak tolong di jawab dong kak secepatnya bahasa Inggris saya sudah di terjemahkancontoh:1. open the door - Do you mind to close the door please​


Jawaban:6. B. Closing

Would you mind closing the door? (Bolehkah kamu menutup pintu?)

7. A. Pass

Can you please pass the rice? (Bisakah kamu memberi nasi?)

8. A. It

I read a book, it was good. (Aku membaca buku, itu bagus.)

9. B. They

I bought dictionaries, they are expensive. (Aku membeli kamus, harganya mahal.)

10. C. Himself

Sami told a lie. He was ashamed of himself. (Sami berbohong. Dia malu pada dirinya sendiri.)

11. C. Yours

This is my car, yours is in Bandung. (Ini mobil saya, punya mu ada di Bandung.)

12. D. Them

Donald and Mary work in my office. I know them very well. (Donald dan Mary bekerja di kantor saya. Saya sangat mengenal mereka.)

13. A. Was written

Those plays was written by Shakespear. (Drama-drama itu ditulis oleh Shakespear.)

14. A. Are going to be explained

The lessons are going to be explained by the lecturer. (Pelajaran akan dijelaskan oleh dosen.)

15. C. Have been returned

The books have been returned by the students. (Buku-buku telah dikembalikan oleh siswa.)

16. A. Could

Could i borrow your car? (Bisakah saya meminjam mobil mu?)

17. B. Can not

They can not go to Washington. (mereka tidak bisa pergi ke Washington.)

18. B. Is able to do

He is able to do the homework himself. (Dia mampu mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya sendiri.)

19. D. Are being prepared

The reports are being prepared by Jehan now. (Laporan sedang disiapkan oleh Jehan sekarang.)

20. C. Being informed

Has she being informed of a proposed increase in our rents by the building superintendent? (Apakah dia mendapat informasi tentang kenaikan yang diusulkan dalam sewa kami oleh pengawas bangunan?)

21. C. Were written

Those sentences were written in the library by Omar. (Kalimat-kalimat itu ditulis di perpustakaan oleh Omar.)

22. A. Are going to be collected

Our assigments are going to be collected by our lecturer. (Tugas kami akan dikumpulkan oleh dosen kami.)

23. D. She lives

I know where she lives. (Aku tau dia tinggal dimana.)

24. B. They are coming

Do you know when they are coming? (Apakah kamu tahu kapan mereka akan datang?)

25. A. Whose pen is this?

I do not know whose pen is this? (Saya tidak tahu pena siapa ini?)
