Secondly may shalawat and Salam always be delivered to our Prophet Nabi Muhammad saw, who had brought us from the darkness to this bright modern era that we live in today. Ladies and gentlemen, good afternoon. My name is Salsabila Ramadhani and I’m from Ten 3 standing here, in front of you all to deliver my speech, under the title of Save Our Culture.

Do you know the meaning of culture? Culture is a life method which grew in social groups and is used throughout generations and generations. As we all know, we have thousands of legacies of culture; which has a high precious of art and an ancestral masterpiece such as music, language, traditional dance, and cuisine.

Along with the development of time, a passion for preserving culture is fading. Why? Because the advancement of science and technology, the impact of globalization, lack of awareness. Seeing other people's culture, many of us experience distaste about our own culture. Like now, there are many teenagers that are affected by globalization. For example, teenagers who immediately check their phones after waking up. Indonesian teenagers now is more into modern pop music, that they sometimes forget about our own traditional Indonesian songs. Foreign culture almost changes the entire life style of Indonesian people. Such as food. There are many dishes outside our country that are popular in Indonesia. For example, burgers, pizza, spaghetti. Besides food, fashion also takes part. Trending fashionable clothes outside of Indonesia like tight clothes, hotpants and semi-finished clothes. It is absolutely not worthy for our nation that upholds modesty. It is ironic, really.

Many of us, the young generation of this nation, don't care. And why is that? In this globalization era, West culture is very easy to enter Indonesia; especially through social media. It almost looks like we feel more confident and comfortable when imitating the west culture. Another case is our culture confession by Malaysia. They claim our culture as theirs. For instance, reog ponorogo, pendet dance, batik, rasa sayange song, and many more. This can be a motivation to us as the young generation to care more about Indonesian culture. We can't just blame the people who stole our culture.

We must save our culture. And where do we start? From ourselves. If it's not us who love our own culture then who else? We must feel proud in showing our pride through our culture. Starting from ourselves, our families, and our surroundings. Culture can't be spread around that easy, moreover in hurry. There are many efforts to perpetuate Indonesias culture, but that efforts aren't maximum yet. Besides creativity, promotion factor has a big role too. Promote it into international level with interesting and unique ideas. So, Indonesian culture has an international confession and difficult to copy. Don’t ever feel bored to make creativity and save Indonesian culture.

That’s all for my speech, I hope my speech can open up our eyes and rise our spirits on to begin loving own cultures. Wassalamualaikum Wr. Wb.
