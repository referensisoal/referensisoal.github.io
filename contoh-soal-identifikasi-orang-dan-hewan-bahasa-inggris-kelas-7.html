Alisha: "Hey Emma!"
Emma: "Hey Alisha, what's up?"
Alisha: "Nothing much."
Alisha: "Is that the cat you were talking about yesterday at school?"
Emma: "Yes, this cat is my pet, her name is Paris."
Alisha: "Pretty name for a pretty cat!"
Emma: "Thanks, my mother picked out her name."
Alisha: "Paris is so cute!"
Emma: "You have to feel her fur!"
Alisha: "Oh wow, it's so soft!"
Emma: "By the way, do you have any pets in your house?"
Alisha: "No, I can't have one."
Emma: "Why not? Not even a hamster or some sort of rodent?"
Emma: "They're small and they're not hard to be taken care of."
Alisha: "Sadly my parents don't want any kind of pets in our house, even the smallest ones."
Emma: "Do you want one though?"
Alisha: "Yeah, I really want to have a pet."
Emma: "Well you can always come over to my house and play with Paris, my cat."
Alisha: "Wow, are you sure, Emma?"
Emma: "Of course!"
Alisha: "Thank you so much!"
