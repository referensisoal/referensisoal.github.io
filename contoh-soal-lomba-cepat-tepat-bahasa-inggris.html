6. cara cepat menjawab soal bahasa inggris gmn ?


1. bila soal bacaan, baca soalnya dl, pahamo maksud soal, kmd cari jawabannya di bacaan yg ada.
2. bila soal rumpang, pahami dl tense yg ada.
3. bila menyusun paragraf, ikuti alurnya sesuai dg kata kunci yg ada di setiap kalimat.
4. bila teks pendek spt notice, announcement, warning, baca soalnya hati2, pahami arti soalnya.
cra cpatnya (jika soalny trdapat text)

1. baca soalny dhulu
2. pahami soalny
3. lalu bca textnya
4. perbanyak vocabulary agk mmbantu untk memahami soal

31. contoh-contoh kata ganti dalam bahasa Inggriscepat ya please


There are five forms of pronoun in english:
1. Subject pronoun : I, you, he, she, it, we, they.
2. Complement pronoun : me, you, him, her, it, us, them.
3. Possessive adjectives : my, your, his, her, its, our, their.
4. Possessive pronouns : mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs.
5. Reflexive pronouns : myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, themselves.

33. contoh recount text bahasa Inggris tentang lomba 17 agustus​


Contoh recount text bahasa Inggris tentang hari 17 Agustus:

The 17th of August is a very special day for Indonesia. It is the day when Indonesia declared its independence from the Dutch colonial rule in 1945. Every year, people celebrate this day with various activities and ceremonies.

On that day, I woke up early and wore my school uniform. I also wore a red and white ribbon on my chest, which are the colors of the Indonesian flag. I went to school with my friends by bike. There was a big flagpole in the center of the yard, where the Indonesian flag was ready to be raised.

We played some traditional games, such as tug-of-war, sack race, and marble race. We also had some competitions, such as singing, dancing, and drawing. The winners received some prizes and certificates. We had a lot of fun and laughter.

I enjoyed the 17th of August event very much. It was not only a celebration, but also a learning experience for me.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah teks yang menceritakan kembali sebuah kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu. Tujuan recount text adalah untuk menghibur atau memberi informasi kepada pembaca. Recount text memiliki tiga bagian: orientasi, rangkaian kejadian, dan reorientasi.
