4. apa arti dari -cuff -collar -pocket -button -zipper


Arti dari cuff adalah manset. Manset adalah bagian ujung lengan baju yang berupa bagian tambahan dan biasanya dibuat kaku. Manset dapat juga berarti kancing pada lengan baju tambahan.

Pembahasan

Arti dari

Cuff = mansetCollar = kerahPocket = sakuButton = kancingZipper = ritsleting

Collar (kerah) adalah bagian dari baju yang terdapat di leher dan biasanya dibuat kaku.

Pocket (saku) adalah bagian dari celana dan baju yang digunakan untuk menyimpan benda-benda. Biasanya saku terdapat di kemeja bagian dada dan di celana bagian samping kanan dan kiri.

Button (kancing) adalah bagian dari baju yang digunakan untuk menutup bagian pakaian. Kancing berpasangan dengan lubang kancing (buttonhole).

Zipper (ritsleting) adalah alat penutup pakaian (baju, celana, dan sebagainya), tas, dan sebagainya terdiri atas deretan gerigi yang terbuat dari logam atau plastik.

Answer:

To answer this kind of question without possibility answer choices we have to understand the whole passage first. After that, fill in the blank part with the answer possible answer which is suitable with the context of the text.

So here’s the possible answer:

1.New

The next sentence tells that the writer just buy the jacket so it’s a new jacket.

2. Good / high-quality.

The next sentence tells the material of the jacket, it generally deals with the quality of a thing.

3. Bright/dark

It deals with the color so it can be bright or dark.

4. Comfortable

The writer describes that the inner part of the jacket is also warm, so the possible answer is comfortable to match with warm.

5. Suitable

The writer describes that the jacket should be worn in cold winter because it’s warm inside, so suitable is the possible answer.

6. Sunny

The writer wants to say that the jacket can be used in any situation. So the possible answer is sunny, because it’s the opposite of cold at the previous sentence.

7. a short time

Because the next sentence is I never zipper it up for a long time. it shows that is the opposite of the previous sentence.

8. only

The writer tells that his size is not available so L is the only size.

9. big

The writer buys the bigger size so it means the jacket is too big.

10. comfortable

The writer tells that although the size doesn’t fit him, he still like to wear the jacket. So the possible answer is comfortable.

In layman’s terms, that’s about seven million zippers each year. So why has no one stepped in and challenged YKK? To put it simply, designers trust the brand and have since 1934. “YKK makes incredibly dependable zippers, ships them on time without fail, offers a wide range of colors, materials, and styles, and never gets badly undercut on price,” writes Seth Stevenson on Slate. And yet surprisingly, YKK did not invent the zipper. That accolade goes to Whitcomb L. Judson, who created the “chain lock fastener” in America in the 1890s. The company still exists as Talon, Inc, and is used by brands such as Brooks Brothers and Uniqlo.
