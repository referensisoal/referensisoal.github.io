5. kunci jawaban bahasa Inggris kelas 7 SMP halaman 147​


The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.The big trees provide shade and oxygen.The cat is cute and playful, she eats a lot of fish but doesn't like rice. Beni feeds the cat.Pembahasan

Kalimat jawaban di atas kita ambil dari dialog sebelumnya tentang berbagai benda dan manusia yang ada di sekitar kita. Pernyataan tersebut berhubungan dengan keberadaan dan fungsi semua benda-benda tersebut.

The sun rises in the east and sets in the west. Karena matahari adalah subject ketiga (it) maka pada verb kita tambahkan -s sesuai kaidah simple present tense, menjadi rises dan sets.The big trees provide shade and oxygen. Pada kalimat ini big trees dalam bentuk plural, berarti sama dengan subject they, maka pada verb tidak kita tambahkan -s menjadi provide.The cat is cute and playful, she eats a lot of fish but doesn't like rice. Dalam hal ini the cat secara umum bila hanya satu akan disebut subject (it), tapi kalau the cat itu sudah dikenal maka bisa disebut she atau he, sesuai jantan atau betina. Beni feeds the cat. 16. buatlah 5 soal bahasa inggris pilihan ganda kelas 3 smp beserta jawabannya?!


Read the caution and answer questions 1 to 4.

CAUTION
VOLCANIC FUMES ARE
HAZAROUS TO YOUR HEALTH AND BE LIFE-
THREATENING
VISITORS WITH BREATHING AND HEART PROBLEMS, PREGNANT WOMEN
AND YOUNG CHILDREN

1. What is the caution about?
A. The volcanoes and their effect
B. The danger of volcanic fumes
C. The women and their children’s health.
D. The pregnant visitors of the mountain.

• Kunci jawaban: B
• Pembahasan:

Dari kata-kata dalam caution seperti hazardous, life-threatening, avoid dapat diketahui bahwa text ini tentang bahaya dari gunung api dimana caution itu dipasang. Hal yang membahayakan itu berasal dari fumes (asap atau gas )yang berbahaya ( hazardous )

2. Visitors with breathing problems are not allowed to visit the area because …
A. the fumes can make them difficult to breathe
B. the fumes can enter the human’s lung
C. it is very tiring to climb the volcano
D. certain people cannot breathe well
• Kunci jawaban: A
• Pembahasan:

Breathing problems dalam soal ini maksudnya orang yang menderita penyakit terkait dengan pernafasan seperti asma. Bagi orang-orang seperti ini menghirup bau yang ditimbulkan gunung api seperti bau blerang bisa membuat mereka tidak mampu bernafas secara wajar bahkan susah bernafas dan efek selanjutnya bisa menimbulkan kematian.

3. Where do you usually find such a caution?
A. At all public places with non domestic visitors
B. Any places visited by tourists.
C. On all the mountains with few visitors
D. At the volcanoes for tourist resorts
• Kunci jawaban: D
• Pembahasan:

Pertanyaan menanyakan informasi tersirat. Dari clue berupa kata seperti volcanic, bisa disimpulkan bahwa caution seperti ini biasa dijumpai di gunung api-gunung api khususnya yang juga berfungsi sebagai tempat wisata.

4. “…. are hazardous to your health ..”
What does the underlined word in the sentence mean?
A. Dreadful.
B. Dangerous.
C. Uneasy.
D. Serious.
• Kunci jawaban: B
• Pembahasan:

Berdasarkan konteks kalimat atau kata-kata lain seperti health=kesehatan, life-threatening= mengancam kehidupan, dan avoid= menghindari, bisa dipastikan bahwa makna kata hazardous sama dengan dangerous, bukan yang lain-lainnya.

33. buat cepen bahasa inggris tentang lomba cerdas cermat


I am a 5th grade student in Beringin Jaya VII Elementary School, I love reading books in the library with my best friend named Zahra and Kirana. At rest time, we prefer to spend some time in the library. Oh yes, I forgot to introduce about myself. My name is Alika. I am the second child of three children. I have an older sister and a younger sister.
While studying in class, suddenly Teacher enters the class and calls me, Zahra and Kirana. Teacher called us because we were the top three in the class. Teacher explained that we will be included in the Great Intelligence at Jabodetabek level. I was very excited at the time because I was able to represent the school to participate in the meticulous race.
The competition is held in 2 weeks, and to fill my time, Zahra and Kirana are more often in the library to reading. I am more active learning than ever because I want to give the best for my school. Teacher always accompanies us to study and give new problems that we have never had before in class. Therefore, we were always late from school because of the extra hours to practice.
The race finally arrived. Intelligent contest is held in Taman Mini Indonesia Indah. I was amazed to see the participants who will follow the race, because very many in number. I, Zahra and Kirana had felt nervous because of the many participants. However, we immediately eliminated that feeling and focused back on our goal of winning the race.
The preliminary round consists of 4 teams at each session. This first round is a session to answer the matter in turns, the second round is a session to answer the question of seizure, and the final round is to compose the highest cans. The higher the can, the higher the value. My team won this preliminary round because we answered many questions and compiled the tallest cans.
Passing the preliminary round I was getting tense because during the semi-final of his opponent increasingly tough and smart. The semifinal round of the system is the same as the preliminary round. We won this round and went into the final round. In the final round, the race system is the same as the preliminary rounds and the semi-finals, plus the wording. I'm very nervous because this part of the word is part of what I have to accomplish by myself. The words I had to write were quite difficult but I was able to solve them. Cooperation is needed in this race to victory.
Arrived during the announcement of the winner, I was very happy because the team from SD Beringin Jaya VII won the 1st champion. Present Continuous Tense

Present Continuous Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian sebagai berikut:

1. Situasi dimana suatu kejadian tengah berlangsung sekarang.

Contoh : Mr. Tony is making design of the house.

2. Situasi dimana suatu kejadian sudah di rencanakan dengan pasti untuk terjadi di masa depan.

Contoh : The class is going to start at 8 AM tomorrow

3. Situasi dimana sebuah perubahan atau perkembangan sedang berlangsung untuk waktu yang lama.

Contoh : My parents is getting older.

Pattern atau formula untuk present continuous tense adalah:

(+) S + is/am/are + V ing

(-) S + is/am/are not + Ving

(?) is/ am/ are + S + Ving?

NB:

is untuk subject He, She, It (singular)

are untuk subject you, we, they (plural)

am untuk subject I.
