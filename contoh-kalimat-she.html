Pengertian Pronoun

Dalam grammar, sebuah pronoun didefinisikan sebagai sebuah huruf atau frasa yang bisa di substitusi untuk noun atau frasa noun, yang mana ketika di ganti disebut antecedent pronoun. Hal ini karena pronoun bisa melakukan apa saja yang noun bisa lakukan. sebuah pronoun bisa menjadi subject, direct object, indirect object, objek preposisi dan lainnya. Tanpa pronoun kita mungkin harus sering mengulangi noun dan hal ini akan membuat omongan dan tulisan kita menjadi berulang-ulang dan juga merepotkan. Kebanyakan pronoun adalah kata-kata yang singkat. seperti:

HeShe(kata yang menggunakan huruf kapital adalah kata keterangannya/adverb)

I REALLY love you.

You are COMPLETELY right.

We ALWAYS go to supermarket every saturday.

They RARELY made mistakes.

She is UNDOUBTEDLY clever.

He is STILL a good father to his family.

It works WELL.

Jawab:

1. Verbal Sentence (yg ada kata kerja)

(+) I go to school everyday

Pembahasan:

Present Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menunjukkan suatu Kegiatan/peristiwa yg rutin/kebiasaan dan menyatakan sebuah fakta (kebenaran umum).

Present Tense terdiri 2 bentuk

1. Verbal Sentence

= kalimat yg terdapat kata kerja

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

(-) S + do/does + not + VERB 1 + O

(?) do/does + S + VERB 1 + O?

Subject + Auxiliary

* I, you, we, they = DO

* He, She, it = DOES

2. Nominal Sentence

= kalimat bentuk nominal yg tak ada kata kerja

(+) S + TO BE (is/am/are) + Adj/Adv/Noun

(-) S + TO BE (is/am/are) + not + adj/adv/noun

(?) TO BE (is/am/are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

Subject + To Be

* I = am

* He, She, it = is

* You, we, they = are

Present Continuous Tense:

1. They are playing football in the park.

2. I am studying for my exam at the moment.

3. The children are watching a movie in the living room.

4. We are having dinner at a restaurant tonight.

5. She is writing an email to her friend right now.

Present Tense:

1. He goes to the gym every morning.

2. They play basketball every weekend.

3. She sings beautifully in the choir.

4. We visit our grandparents once a month.

5. The sun rises in the east.

Penjelasan:

1. Present Continuous Tense: This tense is used to describe an ongoing action happening at the present moment. It is formed using the verb "to be" (am, is, are) followed by the present participle (-ing form) of the main verb. For example, in the sentence "They are playing football in the park," the act of playing football is happening right now.

2. Present Tense: This tense is used to describe general truths, habitual actions, or regular occurrences in the present. It is formed by using the base form of the verb (without any additional endings or auxiliaries). For example, in the sentence "He goes to the gym every morning," the action of going to the gym is a habitual activity that happens regularly.

Both tenses refer to actions in the present, but the Present Continuous Tense emphasizes ongoing actions happening at the moment, while the Present Tense describes general truths or habitual actions.
