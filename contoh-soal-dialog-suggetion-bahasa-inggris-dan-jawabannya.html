7. Buatlah percakapan bahasa inggris beserta artinya dan yang didalamnya terdapat kalimat suggetion,offering dan opinion


Adi : "What happened Frank? You don't look well."

Frank : "Really?"

Adi : "Yes, what happened? You can tell me."

Frank : "Actually, I lost my new phone. I have been searching it from yesterday but haven't found it yet. What should I do?"

Adi : "Where did you lose it?"

Frank: "At school."

Adi : "I suggest you to try going to the teacher office." (Suggestions)

Frank : "I did. But they said they didn't find anything. Where do you think I should searched next?"

Adi : "Have you asked other students?"

Frank : "No, I haven't."

Adi : "Then I think it's possible that someone have found it but don't know what to do with it." (Opinion)

Frank : "Really? Thanks I'll start asking around then."

Adi : "Wait!"

Frank : "What's up?"

Adi : " I could help you out if you want." (Offering)

Frank : "Really? Thanks!"

A. If you keep on making mistakes with your homework, you should try the following:

Review your notes and textbook to make sure you understand the concepts and material covered in your homework.

Ask for help from your teacher or classmates if you are having trouble with a particular topic or problem.

Take your time and focus on one problem at a time, rather than trying to rush through all of your homework.

Use different study strategies, such as re-writing notes, using flashcards, or teaching the material to a friend, to help you understand and remember the material better.

B. If you feel nervous before an exam, you should try the following:

Take deep breaths and try to relax your body.

Remind yourself that being nervous is normal and that everyone feels anxious before exams.

Set realistic goals and expectations for yourself, and focus on the things you can control, such as how well you have prepared and studied for the exam.

Avoid comparing yourself to others and try to focus on your own performance.

Eat a healthy meal and get a good night's sleep before the exam to help you feel calm and focused.
