3. Buatlah dialog bahasa Inggris untuk 2 orang menggunakan rumus simple future, future continuous, future perfect (future plans)​


A: “What are you doing?”

B: “I’m working on the agenda for tomorrow’s meeting.”

A: “What will you do tomorrow?” (pertanyaan untuk future tense)

B: “I will meet Sue for lunch to discuss the contract.”

A: “That’s interesting. What contract will you two discuss?”

B: “We are working in the garden most of the weekend. So we are planning to make a business about the interior of small garden in urban city.”

A: “So exciting! I hope that your plan will go well.”

B: “Thank you!”

34. Dialog b. inggris tentang talking about life events ada  yang tau?


(A) When is your birthday? (Kapan kamu berulang tahun?)
(B) December 29(th)

(A) What year? (Tahun berapa?)
(B) That’s personal. (Itu rahasia)

(A) Were you born and raised here? (Apakah kamu lahir dan besar disini?)
(B) No. I wasn’t. (Tidak)

(B) Yes, he did. (Iya)
