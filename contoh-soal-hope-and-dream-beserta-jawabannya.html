19. rumus suggestion and offering , hope and dream and examples please :D


suggestion : subject + verb + that + object
(i propose that a report should be sent in)
offering : modal verb + subject + object
(shall we take you there)
hope : subject + verb + object/complement
(i hope rahmad found the place)
