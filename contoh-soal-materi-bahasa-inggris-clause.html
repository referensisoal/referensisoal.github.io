4. jelasin materi adjective clause dan adverb clause


Adjective clause atau relative clause adalah dependent
clause yang berfungsi sebagai adjective dan
menjelaskan tentang noun atau pronoun pada main
clause dari suatu complex sentence (kalimat yang terdiri
dari independent clause dan satu atau lebih dependent
clause ).
Posisi adjective clause selalu
mengikuti noun atau pronoun yang diterangkannya. Di
dalam kalimat, noun atau pronoun itu berfungsi
sebagai subject atau object. Adjective clause dimulai
dengan suatu kata yang disebut relative pronoun (who,
whom, that, which, whose, etc) yang
berfungsi menjembatani hubungan dengan noun atau
pronoun yang diterangkannya.

9. artikel bahasa inggris yg mengandung noun clause


The problem was that she left early. The noun clause here, “that she left early,” functions as a complement of the subject “problem” in the independent clause. The subject-verb combination of this noun clause is “she left,” and the subordinator is “that.” The subject-verb combination of the independent clause is “problem was.”.

30. membuat teks bahasa inggris yang di dalamnya terdapat noun clause, adjective clause, adverbial clause


The company, which has been in business for over 20 years, has a reputation for producing high-quality products. The employees, who are highly trained and experienced, work diligently to ensure customer satisfaction. The factory, located in the industrial area, has state-of-the-art equipment and technology. The products, that are sold at competitive prices, are in high demand. The company's success, despite the economic downturn, is a testament to its strong management and dedicated staff.

The company, which was founded in 2000, has experienced rapid growth in recent years.

(Noun Clause: which was founded in 2000)

The employees, who were highly motivated, were able to meet their sales targets.

(Adjective Clause: who were highly motivated)

The CEO, after reviewing the financial reports, decided to expand the business.

(Adverbial Clause: after reviewing the financial reports)

The new product, designed to meet customer needs, was launched with great success.

(Adjective Clause: designed to meet customer needs)

The team, despite facing many challenges, managed to complete the project on time.

(Adverbial Clause: despite facing many challenges)

33. sebutkan pengertian dari conjunction-clause dalam bahasa inggris?​


Jawaban:

Pengertian Conjunction

Conjunction adalah kata atau kelompok kata yang berfungsi menghubungkan dua konstruksi gramatikal yang sama yaitu: kata, frasa (phrase), atau klausa (clause). Kata atau kelompok kata ini dapat dibedakan menjadi coordinate, correlative, subordinate, dan adverbial conjunction (subjunctive adverb).

Contoh Kalimat Coordinate Conjunction:

1 I love running and swimming.

(Saya suka berlari dan berenang.)

2 She wants a house, but she can’t afford it.

(Dia menginginkan sebuah rumah, tetapi tidak dapat membelinya.)
