23. contoh dialog tentang menawarkan , menolak dan menerima sesuatu dalam bahasa inggris dalam kehidupan sehari hari


Answer:

Offering and Accepting

A: Is there anything I can do to help?

B: Hmm... I think not at the moment, thank you.

A: Really? You look kind of busy

B: Oh! There is one thing you could do. Please bring the old boxes from the second floor here.

A: Got it.

B: Thank you.

Refusing offer

A: Those boxes look heavy. Would you like me to help?

B: I'm okay. You can hold the door for me though.

A: Okay. Can I take a box from you?

B: No, it's okay. It's actually not that heavy.

A: Is there anything else I can do?

B: Everything is fine. Thanks for offering though.

Detail tambahan

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kategori: Accepting and Refusing offer

Pembahasan:

Sebelum membuat dialog, sebaiknya kita mengetahui kalimat seperti apa yang biasanya digunakan untuk menawarkan (offering), menerima tawaran (accepting an offer), dan menolak tawaran (refusing an offer).

1. Expression of offering

Expression of offering merupakan suatu ekspresi yang digunakan untuk menawarkan suatu benda atau bantuan.

Berikut contoh expression of offering:

Expression of offering help:

- Can I help you?

- What can I do for you?

- Would you like me to ...?

- Can I lend you a hand with that?

Expression of offering things:

- Would you like some ...?

- Would you like something to ...?

- Should I get you ...?

- What can I get for you?

2. Responding an offer

Untuk merespon sebuah tawaran kita dapat menerima atau menolaknya. berikut adalah ekspresi yang dapat digunakan untuk menerima dan menolak tawaran.

Expression of accepting an offer:

- Yes, Please.

- Thanks, I'd love to.

- That sounds nice.

- Thank you, I appreciate it.

Expression of refusing an offer:

- No, Thanks.

- Thank you, but I can do it myself.

- I appreciate it, but I can do it myself.

- Not for me, thanks.

- It's alright I can do it myself, thanks.

Berikut contoh dialog menerima suatu tawaran:

Waiter: Sorry for making you wait, sir. Here is the Banana pancake that you order.

Guest: It's okay. Thank you.

Waiter: Would you like some honey on your pancake, sir?

Guest: Yes, please.

Waiter: Alright. Wait a minute, please. I'll get some honey for you.

Berikut contoh menolak suatu tawaran:

Tony: Mom, you look tired. can i help you with the housework?

Tony's mother: It's alright, Tony. I can do it myself. It's better for you to do your homework.

Tony: Alright, mom.
