9. contoh 10 soal pilihan ganda tentang offering help beserta jawabannya


Offering Something (Menawarkan Sesuatu)

Ini adalah ungkapan yang disampaikan untuk menawarkan suatu barang, bantuan, atau pertolongan kepada orang lain. Dalam bahasa Inggris, contoh ekspresi yang sopan untuk menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain adalah:

* Your bag looks heavy. May I help you carrying it? (Tas Anda kelihatannya berat. Bisakah saya bantu membawanya?)

* Would you like a cup of hot tea? (Anda mau secangkir teh panas?)

* I have just bought a good book, would you like to read it too? (Aku baru saja beli buku yang bagus, kamu ingin membacanya juga?)

Sedangkan Offering Help adalah menawarkan yang secara spesifik tentang bantuan yang bisa diberikan pada orang lain. Misalnya:

* Is the English homework difficult? Let me help you doing it. (Apakah PR Bahasa Inggrisnya sulit? Akan kubantu mengerjakan)

27. apa perbedaan antara offering for help dan asking for help


Offering for help and asking for help are two different actions related to seeking assistance or support, but they involve different perspectives and roles.

1. Offering for Help:

When someone offers help, they are taking the initiative to provide assistance or support to another person without being asked. It shows their willingness to extend a helping hand voluntarily. Offering help demonstrates kindness, empathy, and a genuine desire to make a positive impact in someone's life. The person offering help often takes on a supportive role and may identify a need or a problem that they believe they can help with.

For example: "I noticed you're struggling with your project. If you need any help, I'm here to assist you."

2. Asking for Help:

On the other hand, asking for help is when someone actively seeks assistance or support from others. This action requires humility and a willingness to admit that they need help to overcome a challenge or fulfill a task. It involves taking the role of a receiver, acknowledging that they cannot handle the situation on their own, and reaching out to others for aid.

For example: "I'm having trouble understanding this topic. Could you please explain it to me?"

In summary, offering for help is about providing support without being prompted, while asking for help is about seeking assistance when one recognizes the need for it. Both actions are essential for fostering positive relationships and building a supportive community where people can rely on each other.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat pengandaian/bersyarat, di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi. Conditional sentence terdiri dari 2 klausa/kalimat, satu kalimat sebagai If clause dan kalimat lainnya sebagai main clause/result clause. If clause menggambarkan kondisinya, dan main clause menggambarkan konsekuensinya.

Conditional tipe 1 [First conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi. [True in the present/future]

Rumus

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Example

If I have some money, I will buy a new book

Conditional tipe 2 [Second conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan kecil terwujud atau diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang. [Untrue in the present/future]

Nominal sentence pada conditional tipe 2 menggunakan tobe "were" untuk subyek tunggal dan jamak.

Rumus :

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Example

If I were you, I would tell the truth

Conditional tipe 3 [Third conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau. [Untrue in the past]

Rumus :

If+S+had+verb3... S+would+have+verb3....

Example

If I had come to your house, your father would have been happy.

30. Buatlah dialog asking and giving suggestion dan offering help tentang liburan


Jawaban:

A: What are you going to do to spend the upcoming holiday? (Apa yang akan kamu lakukan untuk menghabiskan liburan yang akan datang?)

B: I’m not sure. I guess I’m just going to stay at home during the holiday. (Aku tidak yakin. Aku rasa aku hanya akan tinggal di rumah saja selama liburan)

A: Me too. I don’t have any plan to do something exciting. I think I’ll be dead out of boredom in this year’s holiday. (Aku juga. Aku tidak punya rencana apapun untuk melakukan hal-hal yang menarik. Sepertinya aku akan mati kebosanan di liburan tahun ini)

B: Hey, why don’t we spend the holiday together? (Hei, bagaimana jika kita menghabiskan liburan bersama)

A: That’s great idea! Do you have any suggestion on where we should go and what we should do on the holiday? (Itu ide yang sangat bagus! Apakah kamu punya saran ke mana kita harus pergi dan apa yang harus kita lakukan sewaktu liburan?)

B: How about we spend our holiday at my grandfather’s cottage? (Bagaimana jika kita menghabiskan liburan kita di pondok kakekku?)

A: What kind of activities can we do there? (Aktivitas macam apa yang bisa kita lakukan di sana?)

B: Anything fun, such as fishing. swimming, surfing, sun-bathing, cycling, strolling around the nature, et cetera. (Apapun yang menyenangkan, seperti memancing, berenang, berseluncur, berjemur di bawah sinar matahari, bersepeda, berjalan-jalan di alam, dan sebagainya)

A: Did you just mention surfing? Is the cottage near a beach? (Apakah kamu barusan menyebutkan berseluncur? Apakah pondoknya dekat dengan pantai?)

B: Yeah. We can walk for about 10 minutes to get to the beach from the cottage. Moreover, it’s also close to a small lake, so that we can catch some fish there. (Ya. Kita bisa berjalan sekitar 10 menit untuk sampai ke pantainya dari pondok. Terlebih lagi, pondoknya juga dekat dengan danau kecil sehingga kita bisa menangkap ikan di sana)

A: Cool! We’d better ask Jane and Joe to come with us too. (Keren! Kita juga harus ajak Jane dan Joe)

B: Agree! I’ll call Jane and you’ll call Joe. (Setuju! Aku akan menelepon Jane dan kamu akan menelepon Joe)

A: Okay. I hope they can come join us. (Oke. Aku harap mereka bisa ikut dengan kita)
