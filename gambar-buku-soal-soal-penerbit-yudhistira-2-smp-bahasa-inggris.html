jmlah rabat buku mat = 30%. (240. 15000) =30% . 3600000 = Rp 1.080.000

jmlah rabat buku ipa = 25% . (300.12000) = 25% . 3600000 = Rp 900.000

harga buku setelah dirabat mat = 240.15000 = Rp 3.600.000 - 1.080.000 = Rp 2.520.000

harga buku setelah dirabat ipa = 300.12000 = Rp 3.600.000 -900.000 = Rp 2.700.000

harga pembayaran seluruhnya = Rp 5.220.000,00

His kindness makes everyone love him even the animals love him too, especially his two friends Gus and Jaq. They want to do anything for his friend Cinderella.
Cinderella lives with her stepmother and two half sisters named Anastasia and Drizella.
In contrast to the good and diligent Cinderella, Anastasia and Drizella are very lazy and jealous. They told Cinderella to do all the work of cleaning up the house, sewing, cooking and gardening.
Although the treatment of both his siblings was very bad but Cinderella tried to do his best and tried to make his stepmother and two half-sisters happy.
Madam Tremaine Stepmother Cinderelaa is a cold, cruel and very envious person with the charm and beauty of Cinderella.
He likes to make cinderella busy with all the work, even the exhausted Cinderella does not pity him. Instead he gave an additional task of asking Cinderella to bathe his naughty cat Lucifer.
One day, a messenger from the palace arrived with a special invitation. There will be a big party in the palace.
The king wants his son immediately looking for a future wife. Every young girl in the kingdom is invited, including Cinderella.
Cinderella is very excited about the party's invitation. In the attic, he found the dress that once belonged to his mother.
The dress was a bit old-fashioned, but with the cleverness Cinderella made the dress to be beautiful!
