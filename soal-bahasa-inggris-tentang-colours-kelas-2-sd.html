38. What colours are secondary colours?


Jawaban:

green(from blue and yellow),orange(from red and yellow),puple(from blue and red)

Jawaban:

Secondary color is the color that results from a mixture of two primary colors in a color space. Examples of secondary colors are orange, (red + yellow), green (yellow + blue), and purple (red + blue).
