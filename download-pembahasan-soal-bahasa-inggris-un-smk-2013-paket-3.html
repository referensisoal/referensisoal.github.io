6. Jawaban halaman 56 Buku paket bahasa inggris kurikulum 2013


Understanding and Associating Sentence StructureJawabanDayu says,"Siti and Lina (1),let's (2) play (3) football (4) tomorrow afternoon. (5)"Siti says,"You (2) can wear (3) a batik shirt or a formal shirt (4). But please (1) don't wear (3) a T-shirt.(4)"Lina says,"For exercise,please (1) run,walk,or ride (3) a bicycle.(4) Don't use (3) expensive tools.(4)"Mr.Adnan says,"Please (1) paint (3) the walls (4) or furnish (3) the desks. (4) But please (1) don't spill (3) the paint on the floor.(4)"Mrs.Ina says,"Everybody,please (1) bring (3) your sewing kits with you (4) on Monday(5). We (2) will learn to sew(3). Don't forget to bring (3) some cloth too(4)."Udin says,"For you health,(1) do not eat (3) too much instant noodles(4). Eat (3) more vegetables or fresh fruits.(4)"Pembahasan

Soal latihan ini melatih pemahaman akan struktur kalimat dasar dalam Bahasa Inggris. Sesuai contoh yang tersedia pada buku, maka dapat diketahui bahwa masing-masing kolom digunakan untuk mengisi bagian tertentu dari suatu kalimat.

Kolom 1 bisa diisi dengan pelengkap kalimat termasuk keterangan waktu yang fungsinya sama dengan kolom 5.Kolom 2 diisi dengan subjek kalimat atau pemulai kalimat (untuk kalimat suruhan)Kolom 3 diisi dengan predikat kalimat yang umumnya berupa kata kerja utama dari kalimat.Kolom 4 diisi dengan objek kalimat beserta keterangannya.

26. Brainly.bhs inggris buku paket kelas 12 smk halaman 23 & 24 kurikulum 2013 edisi revisi 2018​


Jawaban:

Bahasa Inggris kelas XII halaman 23 dan 24 kurikulum 2013 edisi revisi 2018 adalah mengenai identify the "if" sentences. Read again the text "Why Don't You Visit Seattle?" and identify the "if" sentence along with its pattern.

1. If you visit Seattle, the first thing to do is feeling the fresh air on your face as you sail to Bainbrige Island on a Washington State Ferry.

Pattern 1: If clause + imperative

2. If you want to enjoy Bainbrige Island, stroll around downtown’s galleries, boutiques, coffee houses and cafes.

Pattern 2: If clause + imperative

3. If you visit Seattle, why don’t you tour Pike Place Market’s produce stands and buy something you’ve never tasted.

Pattern 3: If clause + suggestion

4. Unless you have allergic to noises, make sure you take time to spot these beloved icons.

Pattern 4: If clause + imperative

5. If you have enough time, tour the numerous art galleries in Friday Harbor.

Pattern 5: If clause + imperative

6. If you visit Seattle, see exciting and experimental works at Chihuly Garden and Glass.

Pattern 6: If clause + imperative

If you visit this city, you should explore the Space Needle and Pacific Science Center.

Pattern 7: if clause + suggestion

7. If you visit Seattle, watch the world’s most sophisticated aircraft be built before your eyes at the Boeing factory in Mukilteo.

Pattern 8: if clause + imperative

8. If you are curious to know about it, you should explore the dynamics of flight and experience new aviation innovation.

Pattern 9: if clause + suggestion

Penjelasan:

Soal di atas adalah mengenai membuat kalimat if yang diikuti dengan imperative (kalimat perintah) atau suggestion (saran). Di sini kita harus bisa membedakan mana yang kalimat perintah dan mana yang kalimat saran dari bacaan Why Don't You Visit Seattle?

Simple Past Tense VS Past Continuous Tense

Simple Past adalah tense yang dipakai saat kejadian tersebut dimulai dan diakhiri di masa lampau. Juga dipakai saat menjadi kejadian kedua yang terjadi "mengganggu" saat kejadian pertama sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Past Continuous adalah tense yang dipakai saat menyatakan kegiatan tersebut sedang dilakukan atau kegiatan tersebut butuh waktu untuk melakukannya (proses) di masa lampau.

Contoh : I was watching TV, when you called me.

Kejadian pertama adalah menonton TV, lalu datang kejadian kedua yaitu menelepon.

I was writing a letter, while my sister was sewing her cloth.

Dua kejadian terjadi bersamaan di masa lampau dan dua-duanya membutuhkan waktu (proses).

I dropped my phone accidentally, when my dad came.

Dua kejadian terjadi bersamaan di masa lampau dan keduanya tidak membutuhkan proses (langsung terjadi begitu saja).

31. tugas bahasa inggris paket kelas 9 halaman 67 kurikulum 2013​


Berikut jawaban bahasa Inggris kelas 9 halaman 67;

Ingredients

Vegetables

200g (2 cups) of bean sprouts.150g (2 cups) of kangkung or spinach.150g (1 cup) of young cassava leaf but it’s optional.150g (1 cup) of long beans that cut in 3-cm length.

Coconut dressing

3 cloves of garlic.2 teaspoon of kencur.1 teaspoon of salt.1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander.1 tablespoon of finely chopped palm sugar.3 kaffir lime leaves (sliced).200g (2 cups) of coarsely grated fresh coconut.4 large red chilli (sliced).

Method

Vegetables

Cook vegetables separately in boiling water. Do not overcook them.Drain the vegetables and place in a large bowl.

Coconut dressing

Process all the ingredients except the grated coconut.Stir in the coconut.Wrap the mixture in banana leaf.Put it in a steamer and cook over boiling water for 30 minutes.Leave to cool to room temperature.Add the coconut dressing to the vegetables and toss thoroughly.Serve at room temperature.

Pembahasan

Recipe atau resep merupakan teks yang menuliskan bahan bahan dan metode membuat makanan atau minuman. Di dalam resep, penulis memberikan penjelasan berupa tahapan bagaimana cara memasak minuman atau makanan.

Recipe atau resep terdiri dari;

The goal atau judul yang merupakan tujuan dari resep.The ingredients atau bahan bahan.The method atau tahapan tahapannya.

39. jawaban paket bahasa inggris kelas 9 hal 8 dan 9 kurikulum 2013 ​


I will lead you to re!ect on what  you are learning now. Complete  the statements in the box. Discuss  it in your group and with me.

Now I know it is important ...

to say “CONGRATULATION” to people for their success, their  achievements, and their good fortunes.

to say “HOPE” to pray for other people’s success,  achievements, and good fortunes.

Pembahasan

Congratulation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan ucapan selamat kepada orang lain atas pencapaian yang telah diraihnya.

Beberapa contoh ucapan congratulation adalah:

"Congratulations for winning basket competition” “This calls for celebrating! Congratulations!” “You did it! So proud of you!” “I knew it was only a matter of time. Well done!” “You’ve worked so hard for this. Congrats!” “This is awesome! You’re awesome! Way to go!” “Sincere congratulations on your hard-earned success.”

Hope adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mendoakan orang lain agar selalu sukses, beruntung, dan mendapatkan pencapaian yang bagus. Contoh kalimat hope adalah:

I hope I can see you soon.I hope you will get a better life.I hope you can be a succesfull person.I hope you become a ne president.I hope you always be lucky.I hope you get well soon
