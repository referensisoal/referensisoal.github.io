5. Cara cpt hafal grammer??


tense memiliki 3 bentuk dasar berdasarkan waktu:
1. present (saat ini) - v1
2. past (lampau) - v2
3. future (akan datang) - will + v1

kemudian dikembangkan sesuai dengan kejadian:
1. continuous (msh berlangsung) - v-ing
2. perfect (sdh berlangsung ttp msh berhubungan dengan saat ini) - v3

dari kelima ini, digabungkan sperti pohon akar.
1. present tense - v1
2. present continuous - am/is/are +v-ing

3. past tense - v2
4. past continuous - was/were + v-ing

5. present perfect - have/has + v3
6. past perfect - had + v3
7. present perfect continuous - have/has + been + v-ing
8. past perfect continuous - had +been + v-ing

9. future - will +v1
10. past future - would + v1

12. Q Bahasa Inggris [+20]Sebutkan macam-macam Grammer! Sebutkan masing-masing 1 contohnya dan terjemahkan! [tex]\blue{ \boxed{ \tt{good luck}}}[/tex]​


1. Simple present tense.

Example : I read a book ( Saya membaca buku ).

2. Present continuous tense.

Example : I'm cooking right now ( Saya sedang memasak sekarang ).

3. Simple past tense.

Example : Dad repaired his car yesterday ( Ayah memperbaiki mobilnya kemarin ).

4. Simple future tense.

Example : I will study at night ( Saya akan belajar pada malam hari ).

is/am/are + verb

present continuous
is/am/are + V-ing
ex : My parents are coming, we should clean up !

past tense
V2

was/were + verb-ing
